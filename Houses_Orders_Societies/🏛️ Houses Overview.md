# 🌙 House Astral Chorus — The Resonant Hearts

**Simple Summary:**  
The Astral Chorus are empaths who can influence emotions and reality through harmony and connection.

The **Astral Chorus** represents the emotional and intuitive core of Saint Radian. Its members are empaths, harmonizers, and resonance-weavers who channel feeling into tangible force.

**Sanctum — The Luminous Atrium:** an open, cathedral-like space filled with soft light, reflective pools, and echoing acoustics designed for harmonic convergence.

- **Occult Specialty:** **Resonance Harmonization** — emotional frequencies woven into sound, allowing members to stabilize minds, amplify bonds, or disrupt hostile forces.
    
- **Signature Ability:** When in synchronized groups (especially 3–4 members), they can produce harmonic waves capable of neutralizing spells or destabilizing constructs.
    
- **Secret:** Their emotional output is highly compatible with **F-Link harvesting**, making them one of the most valuable—and exploited—houses in the system.
    
- **Rivalry:** Deep ideological conflict with **House Dialectica**, who dismantle emotion into logic and disrupt their ability to synchronize.

# 🌿 House of Living Sigils — The Garden of Becoming

**Simple Summary:**  
They grow magic like living organisms—blending nature, symbols, and biology into powerful, evolving systems.

The **House of Living Sigils** are bio-mystics who cultivate living glyphs within organic systems. They merge nature and magic into self-sustaining, evolving constructs.

**Dormitory — The Verdant Spire:** a towering greenhouse structure where rooms breathe, vines pulse with sigils, and the environment itself is alive.

- **Occult Specialty:** **Bio-Sigil Grafting** — embedding glyphs into plants, fungi, and living tissue to create regenerative wards, toxins, or adaptive defenses.
    
- **Unique Trait:** Their systems predate modern F-Link architecture, making them partially resistant—or invisible—to its influence.
    
- **Secret:** Advanced members are developing **hive-linked ecosystems**, where multiple organisms share awareness through sigil networks.
    
- **Rivalry:** Quiet tension with **Aetherion Society**, whose mechanical systems cannot fully replicate or control living magic.

# 🏛️ House Dialectica — The Debaters

**Simple Summary:**  
They break everything down—arguments, emotions, even reality itself—until only truth (or something close to it) remains.

**House Dialectica** represents logic, analysis, and the deconstruction of belief. Its members are analysts, strategists, and cognitive tacticians who dismantle arguments and expose hidden structures.

**Forum — The Axiom Hall:** a tiered debate chamber where arguments are dissected in real-time and no idea goes unchallenged.

- **Occult Specialty:** **Cognitive Deconstruction** — breaking down emotional, symbolic, or narrative constructs into analyzable components.
    
- **Signature Ability:** They can destabilize opponents by stripping meaning and coherence from their arguments, often leaving them unable to “anchor” their stance.
    
- **Secret:** Their methods can unintentionally mirror the same reduction systems used by F-Link, making them dangerously aligned with its logic.
    
- **Rivalry:** Ongoing conflict with **Astral Chorus**, whose emotional synchronization collapses when subjected to Dialectica’s analysis.

# 🜂 House Obsidian Glyphs — The Keepers of Inscribed Truth

**Simple Summary:**  
They write reality using symbols—ancient, precise, and extremely powerful.

The **House of Obsidian Glyphs** are masters of symbolic inscription. They believe reality is governed by meaning, and that properly constructed symbols can bind, define, or reshape existence.

**Archive — The Black Library:** a vast, shadowed repository of etched stone, floating glyphs, and forbidden inscriptions.

- **Occult Specialty:** **Glyph Inscription** — encoding reality through symbols that act as laws, contracts, or constraints on the world.
    
- **Signature Ability:** They can bind entities, reinforce structures, or rewrite localized rules using layered glyph systems.
    
- **Secret:** Some glyphs predate Saint Radian—and even the current system—hinting at a deeper, older authority.
    
- **Rivalry:** Constant ideological clashes with **Aetherion Society**, who treat reality as code rather than meaning.

# 🕶️ House of the Liminal Veil — The Architects of Perception

**Simple Summary:**  
They control what people see—and more importantly, what they _don’t_ see.

The **House of the Liminal Veil** specializes in perception, stealth, and cognitive manipulation. Its members operate in the spaces between awareness, altering reality by shifting what is noticed.

**Domain — The Veil Corridors:** a labyrinth of shifting architecture, mirrored spaces, and perceptual distortions where orientation itself is unreliable.

- **Occult Specialty:** **Perceptual Manipulation** — altering attention, memory, and sensory interpretation to hide, misdirect, or erase presence.
    
- **Signature Ability:** They can move undetected not by invisibility, but by removing themselves from conscious recognition.
    
- **Secret:** Their techniques overlap dangerously with the **Janglers’ narrative control methods**, suggesting deeper ties to systemic enforcement.
    
- **Rivalry:** Viewed with suspicion by nearly all Houses—especially **Aeonis**, whose power depends on being seen and recognized.
