# Saint Radian Houses / Orders

---

houses:

  - name: 🜂 House of the Obsidian Glyphs — The Keepers of Inscribed Truth
    focus: Occult linguistics, etymology, sigil-crafting, narrative inscriptions
    specialization: Light & dark glyph systems, beast-binding scripts, ciphered communication
    motto: "Truth hides in etchings."
    dorm: The Sepulchre
    colors: ["#0B0B0D", "#2C1E3F", "#C8A96A", "#E8E4D8"]
    status: Fearful, secretive, academically prestigious
    signature_beasts: [Harpies, Centaurs, Yatagarasu]
    apex: Aurelian Voss — "The Grand Inscriber"
    rivals: [The Aetherion Society]
    allies: [House Dialectica]
    domain: Meaning → Inscription

  - name: ⚙️ House of the Aetherion Society — The System Architects
    focus: Techno-forging, AI systems, quantum machines
    specialization: Relic engineering, containment tech, proto-A.I. offshoots of F-Link
    motto: "Systems build futures."
    dorm: Titan's Workshop
    colors: ["#4DA6FF", "#00E5FF", "#1F2329", "#B0BEC5"]
    status: Popular, pragmatic, sometimes naïve
    signature_beasts: [Phoenix, Griffon, Green Dragon]
    apex: Caelum Virex
    rivals: [Order of the Obsidian Glyphs]
    allies: [House Dialectica]
    domain: Systems → Reconstruction
    secret: Research is unwittingly siphoned into F-Link by the Order
    war_trigger: The Aetherforge Program — reducing spellcraft to code

  - name: 🌙 The Astral Chorus — Empathic Resonance Order
    focus: Jungian analysis, empathy studies, astral projection
    specialization: Oneiromancy, archetypal resonance, proto-telepathy
    motto: "Mind is matter."
    dorm: The Lyceum
    colors: ["#B8A9D9", "#D6D9E6", "#6E7CA6", "#E6B7C3"]
    status: Mocked outwardly, feared secretly
    signature_beasts: [Kitsune, Yatagarasu, Phoenix]
    apex: The Four Sopranos (harmonic entity composed of four)
    rivals: [House Pyraxis (Order of the Crimson Flame)]
    allies: [House of Living Sigils, House of the Liminal Veil]
    domain: Emotion → Resonance

  - name: 🔥 House Pyraxis — The Crimson Flame
    focus: Warrior traditions, combat dueling, beast essence channeling
    specialization: Martial augmentation with beast archetypes, ritual tournaments, combustion manifestation
    motto: "Only those who burn, endure."
    dorm: The Crucible
    colors: ["#8B0000", "#FF4500", "#1C1C1C", "#FFB347"]
    status: Feared, respected
    signature_beasts: [Manticores, War Dragons, Cyclopes]
    apex: Rhazek Valen — "The Unbroken Flame"
    rivals: [The Astral Chorus, House Dialectica]
    allies: [House Aeonis]
    domain: Will → Combustion / Catalyst
    city_node: Cinderfall District

  - name: 🕶️ House of the Liminal Veil — The Architects of Perception
    focus: Espionage, infiltration, perception-control
    specialization: Illusions, shapeshifting archetype studies, memory manipulation, narrative engineering
    motto: "Between shadow and light, there is power."
    dorm: Nadir Hall
    colors: ["#0A0A0A", "#1A1F3A", "#5B4B8A", "#C0C3FF"]
    status: Distrusted, feared, used as enforcers by faculty
    signature_beasts: [Kitsune, Harpies, Mirror Serpents]
    apex: Veyra Null — "The Manyfold Veil"
    rivals: [All Houses — universally distrusted]
    allies: [The Astral Chorus]
    domain: Perception → Distortion
    higher_power: The Furies — serve as their instruments of narrative enforcement

  - name: 🌿 House of Living Sigils — The Garden of Becoming
    focus: Botanical occultism, alchemy, bio-sigil growth
    specialization: Growing living sigils in vines, beast-aligned herbal enchantments, bio-symbolic synthesis
    motto: "Every root holds memory."
    dorm: The Verdant Spire
    colors: ["#2F6F4F", "#4CAF7A", "#A3B18A", "#7FFFD4"]
    status: Quiet, neutral wild card
    signature_beasts: [Dryads, Hydra, Treants]
    apex: Eidren Solvale — "The Verdant Sovereign"
    rivals: [Neutral — occasional tension with House Pyraxis]
    allies: [The Astral Chorus]
    domain: Life → Evolution
    spiral_codex: Embodies the Spiral Codex architecture — predates F-Link

  - name: ♾️ House Aeonis — House of Eternal Soul
    focus: Narrative leadership, charisma projection, solar archetypes, divine mandate studies
    specialization: Photokinesis (light manipulation), aura projection, purification rituals
    motto: "In Radiance, We Ascend."
    dorm: The Empyrean Spire
    color: "#FFD700"
    status: Elite, prestigious, publicly revered, secretly arrogant
    signature_beasts: [Phoenix, Griffon, Solar Drakes]
    rivals: [House of the Liminal Veil, Order of the Obsidian Glyphs]
    allies: [House Pyraxis (Order of the Crimson Flame)]

  - name: 🏛️ House Dialectica — The Dialectic Order
    focus: Logical debate, narrative deconstruction, emotional detachment, rhetorical strategy
    specialization: The Cognitive Shift, Elenchus (Socratic refutation), Strawman Dismantling, Compression Framing
    motto: "Through Contradiction, Clarity."
    dorm: The Agora
    color: "#808080"
    status: Respected, intimidating, seen as emotionally cold
    signature_beasts: [Sphinx (intellect), Golem (logic), Yatagarasu (truth)]
    rivals: [House Pyraxis (Order of the Crimson Flame)]
    allies: [The Aetherion Society, Order of the Obsidian Glyphs]
    domain: Truth → Deconstruction
