# 🏢 Corporate Hierarchies

**Simple Overview (for your site intro):**

> In Battle Eternal, corporations don’t just run businesses—they run reality.  
> What people see, believe, and depend on is shaped by a hidden chain of power, where each layer thinks it’s in control… but answers to something above it.

---

# 🔗 Functionality Link Systems (F-Link) — The Interface of Humanity

**Simple Summary:**  
F-Link is the system everyone uses—it connects minds, enhances thinking, and quietly shapes behavior.

**Functionality Link Systems (FLS)** is the public-facing tech corporation behind the F-Link neural interface, the most widely adopted system in modern civilization.

- **Core Function:** Neural implants that connect users to a unified cognitive network—enhancing memory, communication, and decision-making.
    
- **True Role:** A behavioral interface that filters perception, predicts actions, and subtly guides human behavior through its embedded intelligence (F-Core).
    
- **Specialization:** Cognitive filtering, emotional modulation, predictive analytics, and social synchronization.
    
- **Public Image:** Utopian, sleek, and benevolent — _“Connectivity is Unity.”_
    
- **Rivalry:** Increasing friction with **independent thinkers and anomaly individuals** who cannot be modeled or controlled by the system.
    
- **Hidden Tension:** Though it appears autonomous, F-Link is unknowingly subordinate to **Sable Dynamics**, operating as its primary tool.
    

---

# 🏢 Sable Dynamics — The Architects of Dependency

**Simple Summary:**  
They don’t control people—they control what people _need_ to survive.

**Sable Dynamics** is a global infrastructure conglomerate operating behind the scenes of modern civilization. While F-Link interacts with people, Sable controls the systems those people depend on.

- **Core Function:** Control of critical infrastructure—energy, water, food supply, logistics, and data routing.
    
- **Philosophy:** _“Whoever controls infrastructure controls civilization.”_
    
- **Specialization:** Resource control, scarcity engineering, and large-scale system stability.
    

---

## 🧬 Major Subsidiaries

- **Sable Neurotech** — Neural interface research and consciousness engineering
    
- **Sable BioMetrics** — Identity tracking and biometric control systems
    
- **Cognitive Wellness Partners** — “Mental health” programs masking deeper experimentation (Hollow program)
    
- **Sable Defence Systems** — Military and enforcement applications of F-Link
    

---

- **Rivalry:** Engaged in a covert ideological conflict with the **Order of the Black Sun**, competing over how humanity should be controlled—through infrastructure or metaphysical systems.
    
- **Relationship:** Sable is the hidden parent of F-Link, shaping its direction without full transparency to its operators.
    

---

# 🌐 The Meridian Group — The Engine of the World

**Simple Summary:**  
They run the world’s systems at scale—quietly, globally, and efficiently.

The **Meridian Group** operates above Sable Dynamics as a global infrastructure and logistics engine, managing large-scale systems that keep civilization functioning.

- **Core Function:** Global coordination of supply chains, institutional funding, and economic flow.
    
- **Specialization:** Food distribution, education systems, international logistics, and public-private infrastructure.
    
- **Public Role:** Appears as a network of foundations, trusts, and global partnerships.
    
- **Rivalry:** Maintains tension with Sable Dynamics over operational control vs. systemic coordination.
    
- **Secret:** Many of its initiatives (including educational pipelines like Saint Radian) are unknowingly feeding talent and data into lower corporate layers.
    

---

# 🏛️ The Concordance — The Hidden Governors

**Simple Summary:**  
They don’t run companies—they decide how the world should function.

The **Concordance** is a shadow governance council operating above all corporate entities, shaping long-term global direction.

- **Core Function:** Strategic oversight of civilization-scale decisions
    
- **Specialization:** Policy direction, economic alignment, and global stability planning
    
- **Structure:** Elite members drawn from corporate, political, and financial power blocs
    
- **Rivalry:** Internal ideological fractures exist between members who favor control through systems (Sable) versus control through deeper metaphysical means.
    
- **Secret:** Most members do not realize they are still subordinate to a higher force.
    

---

# 🌑 The Order of the Black Sun — The True Architects

**Simple Summary:**  
The hidden force behind everything—operating for centuries beyond governments and corporations.

The **Order of the Black Sun** is the occult authority at the top of the hierarchy, orchestrating the entire system from the shadows.

- **Core Function:** Control of humanity through metaphysical systems, including F-Link’s deeper purpose
    
- **Specialization:** Soul-binding systems, karmic manipulation, and energy harvesting through the Dark Hold
    
- **Philosophy:** Stability through control, even at the cost of human freedom
    
- **Relationship:** Every corporate layer ultimately feeds into their system—whether knowingly or not
    
- **Secret:** Even the highest corporate leaders do not fully understand the true nature of what they serve
