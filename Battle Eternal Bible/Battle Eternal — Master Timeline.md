# Battle Eternal — Master Timeline

## Mythic Age

Formation of the Dreamfield, emergence of pantheon archetypes, and the birth of the Primal Wilds.

Ancient mystics establish the Lex Somni treaty to prevent mythic forces from fully entering physical reality.

---

## Hidden Age

Secret societies begin studying archetypal resonance.

The Order of the Black Sun forms and discovers Inscription Arcana.

Early Terra Trion Gate theory is developed.

---

## Technological Age

Modern technology enables the Order’s plans.

F-Link is created as a global consciousness harvesting network.

Saint Radian Academies are constructed as vertices of a planetary inscription ritual.

Dark Hold is developed to store emotional resonance data.

---

## Awakening Age

### Season 1 — The Invisible Machine

The Centaur Incident reveals Dreamfield instability.

Alexander’s resurrection disrupts the Order’s control systems.

Riven Takar begins his rise as a manipulative antagonist.

The Quantum Fracture forms.

---

### Season 2 — The Primal Awakening

Mythic beasts from the Primal Wilds manifest worldwide.

Champions begin forming bonds with these creatures.

The Order begins harvesting mythic beings for energy.

Pantheon hosts begin awakening.

---

### Season 3 — The Pantheon War

Pantheon hosts emerge globally.

The Order attempts to activate the Terra Trion Gate.

Neo-Osaka becomes the epicenter of mythological conflict.

The battle for humanity’s narrative begins.
