# Saint Radian Faction Structure

Each House corresponds to a different layer of power within the Battle Eternal cosmology.

---

## Technology Layer

House: [[The Aetherion Society]]

Belief:  
Reality can be engineered through machines and code.

Key Programs:

- [[The Aetherforge Program]]
    
- relic containment systems
    
- proto-AI spell interfaces
    

---

## Consciousness Layer

House: [[The Astral Chorus]]

Belief:  
Mind and archetype shape reality.

Key Fields:

- Oneiromancy
    
- Dreamfield research
    
- empathy resonance
    

---

## Narrative Control Layer

House: [[House of the Liminal Veil]]

Belief:  
Truth belongs to those who control perception.

Key Fields:

- illusion magic
    
- memory alteration
    
- covert operations
    

---

## Natural Magic Layer

House: [[House of Living Sigils]]

Belief:  
Magic grows organically through nature.

Key Fields:

- bio-sigils
    
- alchemical flora
    
- beast-aligned herbs
    

---

## Linguistic Magic Layer

House: [[Order of the Obsidian Glyphs]]

Belief:  
Reality is written in symbolic language.

Key Fields:

- runic inscriptions
    
- sigil coding
    
- beast-binding scripts
    

---

## Mythic Leadership Layer

House: [[Order of the Eternal Sol]]

Belief:  
Reality is shaped by narrative authority.

Key Fields:

- photokinesis
    
- aura projection
    
- ideological leadership
    

---

## Martial Power Layer

House: [[Order of the Crimson Flame]]

Belief:  
Strength is the ultimate purifier.

Key Fields:

- ritual dueling
    
- beast essence channeling
    
- combat trials
