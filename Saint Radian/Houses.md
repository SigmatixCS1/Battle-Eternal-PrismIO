# Saint Radian Houses & Orders

Saint Radian Academy is divided into seven major Houses and Orders.

Each represents a **distinct philosophy of power**, shaping how students approach magic, technology, leadership, and mythic forces.

The Houses act as both:

• dormitory societies  
• academic specializations  
• ideological factions

They also serve as a **microcosm of the wider philosophical conflict shaping the world.**

# Hidden Function of the Houses

Officially the Houses exist to foster specialization and excellence.

Secretly they function as **experimental philosophical ecosystems** used by the [[Order of the Black Sun]] to study how different power ideologies interact.

Students unknowingly participate in a large-scale sociological and metaphysical experiment.
