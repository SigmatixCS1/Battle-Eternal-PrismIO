# Saint Radian Sorting System

Incoming students undergo a ceremonial evaluation known as **The Resonance Trial**.

The trial determines which House their psyche aligns with.

---

# The Resonance Trial

Students are exposed to a controlled Dreamfield interface.

During the process their consciousness interacts with several archetypal stimuli.

These responses reveal:

• emotional resonance  
• archetypal affinity  
• cognitive patterns  
• mythic compatibility

---

# Assignment Outcomes

The student is then assigned to the House whose philosophy best matches their internal structure.

Examples:

Technological thinkers → Aetherion Society

Empathic dreamers → Astral Chorus

Strategic manipulators → Liminal Veil

Naturalistic mystics → Living Sigils

Symbolic scholars → Obsidian Glyphs

Charismatic leaders → Eternal Sol

Warriors → Crimson Flame

---

# Hidden Truth

Unknown to students, the Resonance Trial also feeds data into the **F-Link psychological analysis network**.

The results are quietly cataloged by the [[Order of the Black Sun]] for long-term behavioral modeling.
