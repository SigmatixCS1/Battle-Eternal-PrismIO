# 🧭 Battle Eternal — Team Workflow & AI Alignment Guide
## Internal Note — For All Contributors

---

## ⚠️ Core Principle

> Understanding comes before execution.

Battle Eternal is not a traditional project where you can jump straight into building or marketing.

At this stage:

> The Obsidian Vault *is* the story.

Everything we create should come from that foundation.

---

## 📚 Start Here

Before contributing:

- Read the relevant Vault materials  
- Understand the concepts you’re working with  
- Be able to explain them in your own words  

If something isn’t clear yet:

> Take time to understand it before building on top of it.

---

## 🤖 AI as a Tool (Not a Shortcut)

AI is part of the workflow.

It can:
- accelerate writing  
- generate ideas  
- help iterate quickly  

But it does not:
- fully understand the project  
- verify accuracy  
- replace foundational knowledge  

---

## 🧠 Internal Understanding vs External Tools

There are two parts to the process:

### Internal (You)
- Understanding  
- Judgment  
- Pattern recognition  
- Alignment with the Vault  

### External (AI Tools)
- Generation  
- Suggestions  
- Iteration support  

---

## ⚖️ Balance

> The most effective workflow is:
> **Understand → Generate → Verify → Refine**

If this order is reversed, mistakes compound.

---

## 🔁 The Feedback Loop to Avoid

Sometimes it can feel productive to:

- generate a lot of AI content  
- review multiple variations  
- keep iterating rapidly  

This creates momentum, but it can also lead to:

- misalignment with the source material  
- introduction of incorrect concepts  
- additional revision work later  

---

## ✅ Productive Flow

- Read first  
- Think and align  
- Use AI to assist  
- Verify against the Vault  
- Then build  

---

## ⚠️ On Speed vs Accuracy

Fast output can feel like progress.

However:

> Accurate alignment early prevents rework later.

Taking time to understand the system reduces the need for corrections.

---

## 🧩 Clarity Over Volume

More content ≠ better content

- A few aligned decisions are more valuable than large amounts of unverified output  
- Precision is more important than quantity  

---

## 🎯 Working as a Team

Each role contributes differently:

- Creative direction ensures alignment with the world  
- Execution brings ideas into reality  
- Strategy helps scale and communicate  

For this to work smoothly:

> Alignment should come before execution.

---

## ⚖️ AI in Context

AI is not new—it’s an evolving tool that is becoming more visible and accessible.

Like any tool:

- It can be used effectively  
- Or it can be misused  

The difference comes from how it’s applied.

---

## 🔑 Practical Guideline

Before using any generated content:

Ask:

1. Does this align with the Vault?  
2. Is this specific to Battle Eternal?  
3. Can I explain this without relying on AI?  

If not:

> Revisit the source material and refine.

---

## 🧠 Closing Thought

> AI can accelerate the process.  
> Understanding determines the direction.

When both are used together intentionally:

- work becomes more efficient  
- alignment improves  
- the final result is stronger  

---

## ✅ Simple Workflow Summary

**Read → Understand → Generate → Verify → Build**
