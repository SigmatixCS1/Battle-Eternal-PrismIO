# Battle Eternal — Cosmology Map

_Metaphysical Structure of the Universe_

---

## Overview

The universe of **Battle Eternal** exists as a layered cosmological system called **The Ladder of Worlds**. Each layer represents a deeper level of reality where emotions, archetypes, and consciousness interact with physical events.

Most humans perceive only the **Surface World**, unaware that their actions generate metaphysical consequences that propagate through deeper layers of existence.

The cosmology functions as a **vertical stack of reality** connected by the flow of moral energy, dreams, and narrative forces.

# Realm I — The Surface World

The Surface World is the physical reality where the story begins.

Key Locations:

- **Neo-Osaka** — the primary megacity of the Hegemony
    
- **Saint Radian Institute** — elite academy and emotional harvesting engine
    
- **Corporate infrastructure** — Sable Dynamics, Meridian Group
    
- **F-Link Neural Network** — global consciousness control system
    

This layer contains:

- modern technology
    
- political systems
    
- surveillance networks
    
- social hierarchies
    

Human choices made here generate **emotional and moral energy** that descends into deeper layers.

---

# Realm II — The Dreamfield

The **Dreamfield** is the collective subconscious of humanity.

Characteristics:

- shared dream environments
    
- symbolic landscapes
    
- archetypal imagery
    
- emotional resonance fields
    

Individuals with psychic sensitivity can navigate this realm.

Examples:

- Selene exploring dream spaces
    
- nightmare entities emerging during instability
    
- dream residue appearing in the Surface World
    

The Dreamfield acts as a **translation layer** between physical reality and mythic forces.

---

# Realm III — The Mythic Strata

The Mythic Strata is where **archetypes become active entities**.

In this layer:

- stories shape reality
    
- symbols hold metaphysical power
    
- mythological forces influence events
    

Entities connected to this realm include:

- Nemesis
    
- archetypal heroes and judges
    
- narrative forces shaping destiny
    

Characters like **Sigmatix** can perceive the structure of this realm directly.

---

# Realm IV — The Fury Domains

The Fury Domains are the cosmic enforcement layer governed by the **Erinyes (Furies)**.

The Three Furies:

|Fury|Domain|
|---|---|
|Megaera|Punishment of betrayal|
|Tisiphone|Punishment of murder|
|Alecto|Punishment of moral corruption|

These entities ensure **cosmic balance** when civilizations become corrupted.

They do not follow human law.  
They follow **cosmic equilibrium**.

When systemic injustice reaches extreme levels, the Fury Domains become active.

---

# Realm V — The Dark Hold

The Dark Hold is the deepest layer of the cosmology.

At its center lies:

**Server Zero**

A metaphysical machine exploited by the **Order of the Black Sun**.

Functions of the Dark Hold:

- accumulate karmic weight
    
- harvest emotional energy
    
- bind souls through metaphysical contracts
    

The Order believes this engine allows them to stabilize civilization indefinitely.

However, they may not fully understand the consequences of activating it.

---

# Flow of Influence

Two currents move through the cosmology.

---

## Downward Flow — Descent of Consequence

Human actions create metaphysical weight.

Surface World  
      ↓  
Dreamfield  
      ↓  
Mythic Strata  
      ↓  
Fury Domains  
      ↓  
Dark Hold

This is the **descent of consequence**.

---

## Upward Flow — Return of Judgment

Metaphysical forces influence reality.

Dark Hold  
      ↑  
Fury Domains  
      ↑  
Mythic Strata  
      ↑  
Dreamfield  
      ↑  
Surface World

This is the **return of judgment**.

# Relationship to the Quantum Fracture

The **Quantum Fracture of 2038** destabilizes the boundaries between these realms.

Consequences include:

- dream entities crossing into reality
    
- archetypes manifesting physically
    
- metaphysical signals influencing technology
    
- human consciousness awakening to deeper layers
    

The fracture marks the beginning of the **largest Nemesis cycle in recorded history**.
