# Dreamfield Cosmology — Concept Overview

The Battle Eternal universe operates through **stacked layers of reality**.

Each layer interacts with the ones above and below it.

Think of it like a **cosmic operating system**:

- deeper layers define the rules
    
- upper layers run the applications
    

The deeper the layer, the **more fundamental its influence on reality**.

# Layer 1 — The Null Author

**Domain:** Entropy / Narrative Termination

The Null Author exists outside reality itself.

It represents the force that **ends failed civilizations or corrupted timelines**.

If reality becomes irreparably distorted, the Null Author can erase the narrative entirely.

This entity is rarely referenced and almost never understood by characters.

---

# Layer 2 — The Cosmic Lattice

**Domain:** Moral Memory of Reality

The Cosmic Lattice is the structural backbone of existence.

It records:

- moral weight
    
- emotional resonance
    
- historical truth
    

Every action performed by conscious beings leaves an imprint here.

This is where **Nemesis Patterns originate**.

---

# Layer 3 — Omega Entities

These are the highest beings that interact directly with reality.

They do not rule the universe.

They maintain its balance.

The five Omega entities are:

|Entity|Domain|
|---|---|
|Sigmatix|Memory and Canon|
|Nemesis|Moral Balance|
|Logos|Meaning and Order|
|Dream Sovereign|Collective Imagination|
|Null Author|Entropic Reset|

Each one maintains a fundamental law of existence.

---

# Layer 4 — The Dream Sovereign

The Dream Sovereign is the **living consciousness of the Dreamfield**.

It is composed of the collective imagination of humanity.

It is responsible for:

- myth generation
    
- archetypal stability
    
- symbolic evolution
    

When the Dreamfield is threatened, the Sovereign activates the **Primal Court**.

---

# Layer 5 — The Dreamfield

The Dreamfield is the interface between human consciousness and the Cosmic Lattice.

Dreams are the mechanism that allows minds to connect to deeper archetypal structures.

Normally the Dreamfield remains separated from physical reality by **Lex Somni**.

When this boundary weakens:

- dreams become physical
    
- myths manifest
    
- archetypes awaken
    

---

# Layer 6 — Pantheon Archetypes

Pantheon archetypes are stable mythic identities that formed through centuries of human belief.

Examples include:

- Helios
    
- Nemesis
    
- Athena
    
- Nyx
    

These archetypes exist within the Dreamfield waiting for compatible hosts.

---

# Layer 7 — Pantheon Hosts

When a human resonates strongly with an archetype, the archetype attaches to them.

This produces a **Pantheon Host**.

Hosts retain their identity but gain amplified archetypal influence.

Examples in Battle Eternal:

|Host|Archetype|
|---|---|
|Castor|Nemesis|
|Crystalis|Athena|
|Selene|Nyx|
|Alexander|Solar / Trickster|

---

# Layer 8 — The Primal Wilds

The Primal Wilds represent **pre-civilization mythic forces**.

These creatures are not pantheon entities.

They are older than human mythology.

Examples:

- Nemean Lion
    
- Cerberus
    
- Kitsune
    
- Hō-ō
    
- Talos
    

They act as the **immune system of the Dreamfield**.

When consciousness is suppressed (such as through F-Link), the Primal Wilds begin manifesting.

---

# Layer 9 — Human Civilization

Human society operates mostly unaware of the deeper layers.

Civilization generates the emotional and symbolic energy that feeds the Dreamfield.

When human systems become corrupt or oppressive, the deeper layers begin reacting.

---

# Layer 10 — Technological Systems

This is where the **Order of the Black Sun** operates.

Technological systems include:

- F-Link
    
- Dark Hold
    
- Inscription Arcana
    
- Saint Radian Academy network
    

These systems attempt to manipulate emotional and narrative structures of humanity.

However, they only operate on the **surface layer of reality**.

---

# Why the Dreamfield Is Destabilizing

F-Link harvesting emotional data creates a distortion.

Emotion is not just psychology.

It is **metaphysical energy** tied to the Dreamfield.

When billions of emotional signals are extracted simultaneously, the Dreamfield begins to fracture.

This creates:

- mythological manifestations
    
- archetype awakenings
    
- pantheon host activations
    

The Order does not fully understand the scale of the forces they are disturbing.

---

# Where Sigmatix Fits

Sigmatix exists between the **Cosmic Lattice and the Dreamfield**.

He is the **memory bridge** between reality and its record.

His presence ensures that even if reality is rewritten, the **truth of what occurred cannot be erased**.

---

# Where Nemesis Fits

Nemesis activates when the Cosmic Lattice detects severe moral imbalance.

It chooses a human vessel capable of enforcing corrective balance.

In Battle Eternal:

**Castor Nightfall becomes the vessel.**

---

# The Central Conflict of the Cosmology

The Order of the Black Sun is attempting to do something unprecedented.

Through Inscription Arcana they are trying to **rewrite the Dreamfield itself**.

If successful, they would replace organic myth with **programmed narrative control**.

This would fundamentally alter the balance between all layers of the cosmology.

Deep narrative IPs usually operate on **four layers simultaneously**:

2. **Systemic Power Structures** – politics, institutions, conspiracies
    
3. **Mythology / Cosmology** – metaphysical rules of the universe
