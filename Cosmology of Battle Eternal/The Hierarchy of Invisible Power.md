# The Nemesis Pattern

Within the hidden cosmology of the Battle Eternal universe exists a recurring phenomenon known as **The Nemesis Pattern**.

When a civilization becomes deeply corrupted by injustice, exploitation, and hidden power structures, reality begins assembling a corrective narrative.

This pattern manifests through archetypal roles embodied by specific individuals.

---

## The Stabilizer

Sigmatix

Maintains narrative equilibrium and prevents systemic collapse before the pattern completes.

---

## The Trickster

Alexander

Introduces disruptive chaos into corrupt systems, exposing their contradictions.

---

## The Arbiter

The Auditor

An unknown figure delivering judgment outside conventional legal systems.

---

## The Tyrant

Cassian Thorne and the Order of the Black Sun

Represents the systemic domination that provokes the Nemesis Pattern.

---

## The Nemesis

The Three Furies

Megaera, Tisiphone, and Alecto embody cosmic justice and appear when imbalance reaches its peak.

---

## The Catalyst

An undefined role responsible for triggering the full activation of the Nemesis Pattern.

Multiple individuals may potentially fulfill this position.

## The Layered Architecture of Reality

In the hidden teachings of the Order of the Black Sun, existence is not a single plane.  
Reality is structured as **interlocking strata of consciousness, myth, and metaphysical infrastructure**.

Most humans perceive only the **Surface World**, unaware that their emotions, dreams, and moral weight feed deeper layers of existence.

The Order calls this system:

**The Ladder of Worlds**

# Realm I — The Surface World

### The Hegemony

This is the ordinary reality where the story begins.

Features:

- modern civilization
    
- technology networks
    
- corporate infrastructure
    
- political systems
    

Human beings believe this world operates through **science and social systems**.

But beneath it lies a hidden metaphysical architecture.

Key elements in this layer:

- Saint Radian Institute
    
- F-Link neural network
    
- Sable Dynamics
    
- Meridian Group
    

The Surface World is where **choices and actions create moral weight**.

Those moral imprints descend into deeper layers.

---

# Realm II — The Dreamfield

### The Oneiromantic Layer

Dreams are not merely psychological.

They exist within a shared subconscious field known as the **Dreamfield**.

Properties:
*collective subconscious ocean*
*archetypal imagery*
*memory echoes*
*symbolic landscapes*

Oneiromancers can:

- navigate dream spaces
    
- extract memory fragments
    
- influence emotional states
    

Characters connected to this layer:

- **Selene**
    
- **Nyx**
    
- advanced Saint Radian researchers
    

The Dreamfield acts as a **translation layer** between reality and myth.

---

# Realm III — The Mythic Strata

### The Domain of Archetypes

Beneath the Dreamfield lies a deeper layer where **narrative structures themselves exist**.

In this realm:
*archetypes are real entities*
*stories shape reality*
*symbols have agency*

Examples of mythic forces:

- Nemesis
    
- the Furies
    
- Judgment archetypes
    
- heroic cycles
    

This realm explains why individuals in the story embody **narrative roles**:
*the trickster*
*the stabilizer*
*the tyrant*
*the judge*

Sigmatix operates partially within this layer because he perceives **narrative structure itself**.

---

# Realm IV — The Fury Domains

### Nemesis Territory

The Fury Domains are the **cosmic enforcement layer of morality**.

This realm houses entities that embody divine retribution.

The Three Furies:
*Megaera — punishment of betrayal*
*Tisiphone — punishment of murder*
*Alecto — punishment of moral corruption*

Their function:
*restore cosmic balance*
*punish injustice*
*correct moral distortion*

They do not act according to human law.

They act according to **cosmic equilibrium**.

When civilization becomes morally distorted, the Furies emerge.

---

# Realm V — The Dark Hold

### The Metaphysical Engine

At the lowest level of the cosmology lies the **Dark Hold**.

This is where:

- karmic weight accumulates
    
- emotional energy is harvested
    
- sins are transferred
    

At its center is **Server Zero**, the hidden machine sustaining the Order of the Black Sun.

Purpose:
*convert human suffering into metaphysical power*
*bind souls through karmic contracts*
*maintain control of the system*

The Order believes this engine allows them to **stabilize civilization indefinitely**.

But they may not fully understand what they have created.

---

# Flow of Influence

Two currents move through the cosmology.

### Downward Flow

Human actions generate moral weight.
*Surface World → Dreamfield → Mythic Strata → Fury Domains → Dark Hold*

This is the **descent of consequence**.

---

### Upward Flow

Metaphysical forces influence reality.
This is the **descent of consequence**.

---

### Upward Flow

Metaphysical forces influence reality.
*Dark Hold → Fury Domains → Mythic Strata → Dreamfield → Surface World*

This is the **return of judgment**.
