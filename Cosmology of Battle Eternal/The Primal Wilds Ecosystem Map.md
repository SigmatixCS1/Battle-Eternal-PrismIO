# The Primal Wilds Ecosystem Map

_(Dreamfield Biome System)_

The **Primal Wilds** exist beyond the Dreamfield boundary — a mythic ecological layer where archetypal life forms evolve and interact.

Unlike the physical world, the Wilds are structured around **symbolic ecosystems** rather than geography.

Saint Radian researchers classify these ecosystems as **Mythic Biomes**.

Each biome produces **different types of archetypal beings**.

---

# Structure of the Primal Wilds

Think of it as a layered system.

Human World  
      ↓  
Dreamfield (symbolic boundary)  
      ↓  
Primal Wilds (archetypal ecosystem)  
      ↓  
Mythic Strata (pure archetypal consciousness)

Most creatures originate in the **Primal Wilds layer**.

---

# The Six Major Biomes of the Primal Wilds

These ecosystems generate most Dreamfield creatures.

---

# 1 — The Verdant Expanse

### Archetype: Nature / Instinct / Harmony

This biome resembles endless mythic forests.

Time flows slowly here.

Creatures from this biome often represent **natural balance and instinct**.

Examples:

- Centaurs
    
- Satyrs
    
- Dryads
    
- Great stags
    
- Ceryneian Hind
    

Behavior:

Creatures from the Verdant Expanse tend to be **territorial but not malicious**.

When pulled into the human world, they panic and attempt to return to their natural domain.

The Centaur in Issue 3 comes from this biome.

---

# 2 — The Ember Steppes

### Archetype: War / Fury / Strength

A volcanic plain constantly reshaped by battle.

Creatures here embody **conflict and dominance**.

Examples:

- Minotaurs
    
- Fire drakes
    
- Hellhounds
    
- Chimera variants
    

These creatures are **much more aggressive when manifested**.

Dreamfield breaches involving this biome often produce **violent encounters**.

---

# 3 — The Moonlit Canopy

### Archetype: Illusion / Trickery / Transformation

This biome exists under perpetual twilight.

Creatures here manipulate perception and identity.

Examples:

- Kitsune
    
- Tanuki
    
- Shadow lynxes
    
- Dream serpents
    

Encounters often involve:

- illusions
    
- reality distortion
    
- deception
    

Students may not even realize they are encountering one.

---

# 4 — The Crystal Abyss

### Archetype: Knowledge / Ancient Memory

A subterranean realm of glowing crystal formations.

Creatures here embody **ancient intelligence and hidden knowledge**.

Examples:

- Basilisks
    
- Oracle serpents
    
- Crystal spiders
    
- Memory wraiths
    

Saint Radian researchers are extremely interested in creatures from this biome.

They may contain **ancient mythic data**.

---

# 5 — The Storm Crown

### Archetype: Divine Authority / Cosmic Order

Floating sky realms where immense winged beings exist.

Examples:

- Phoenix / Hō-ō
    
- Thunderbirds
    
- Griffins
    
- Seraphic beasts
    

These creatures are extremely rare in Dreamfield breaches.

Their appearance usually indicates **major cosmic imbalance**.

---

# 6 — The Veiled Depths

### Archetype: Fear / Chaos / Abyss

A dark ocean-like biome beneath the Wilds.

Creatures from this region are deeply connected to **collective fears**.

Examples:

- Leviathan fragments
    
- nightmare kraken
    
- abyssal watchers
    
- dream parasites
    

Breaches involving this biome often produce **psychological horror events**.

---

# Why Creatures Cross Into the Human World

Three conditions must align:

Dreamfield instability  
+  
Archetypal resonance  
+  
Human emotional amplification

This is why Saint Radian is a hotspot.

The academy constantly produces **high emotional output**, weakening the boundary.

---

# The Saint Radian Breach System

Researchers secretly track these events.

They classify them as:

|Breach Type|Cause|
|---|---|
|Emotional surge|student conflicts|
|Dream resonance|powerful dreamers like Selene|
|Archetype convergence|mythic forces aligning|

The Centaur incident was a **Type II Breach**.

---

# What Happens to the Creatures

When a Primal Wilds creature appears in the human world:

1. It becomes physically unstable
    
2. The environment drains its energy
    
3. It attempts to return to its home biome
    

If trapped too long, it **dies**.

Which is why Elara feels the Centaur's suffering.

The world is literally killing it.

---

# The Hidden Saint Radian Agenda

Officially:

> Dreamfield creatures must be contained.

Unofficially the Order believes these beings can be used for:

- archetype research
    
- power extraction
    
- mythic weapon development
    
- Dreamfield navigation
    

This explains why the Centaur’s body later appears **mutilated**.

Someone took it.

---

# The Ecological Balance

The Primal Wilds are normally stable.

But increasing Dreamfield breaches suggest something is wrong.

Sigmatix eventually theorizes:

Archetypal balance destabilizing  
↓  
Dreamfield thinning  
↓  
Wilds bleeding into reality

Which suggests a much larger cosmic shift is happening.
