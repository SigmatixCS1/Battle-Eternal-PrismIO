New knowledge spreads.

Civilization reaches a new state.

Examples:

- empire formation
    
- industrial society

The Spiral Codex Diagram is a symbolic map of the historical cycles governing the rise and collapse of civilizations.

It combines several systems of interpretation:

- the historical spiral of civilizations
- the alchemical transformation cycle
- Nemesis correction events
- interference attempts by the Order of the Black Sun

Together these layers describe the hidden architecture governing human history.

---

The outer spiral begins with ancient civilizations and tightens toward the present.

Major cycles include:

Atlantis (~9600 BCE)

Bronze Age Collapse (~1200 BCE)

Fall of Rome (476 CE)

Black Death Reset (1347 CE)

Industrial Revolution (1800–1900)

Digital Age (1990–2038)

The spiral compresses toward the central node.

---

# Alchemical Layer

Each civilizational cycle follows an alchemical transformation process.

Nigredo — corruption and decay

Albedo — purification and collapse

Citrinitas — awakening and discovery

Rubedo — transformation and renewal

Once Rubedo reaches imbalance, the cycle returns to Nigredo.

---

# Nemesis Activation Nodes

Certain points in history represent Nemesis correction events.

These moments occur when civilizational imbalance reaches critical thresholds.

Examples include:

Atlantis collapse

Bronze Age collapse

Black Death

Quantum Fracture

Each event resets the spiral while allowing civilization to evolve to the next stage.

---

# Black Sun Interference Points

The Order of the Black Sun attempts to intervene at key moments in the spiral.

Their most advanced intervention occurs in the Digital Age through the development of the F-Link neural integration network.

At the center of the Spiral Codex Diagram lies the convergence point.

This node represents the event known as the **Quantum Fracture**.

The fracture marks the moment when the boundary between the Surface World and deeper cosmological layers begins collapsing.

This event initiates the central conflict of the Battle Eternal universe.

Civilizations ascend the spiral through knowledge and ambition.
