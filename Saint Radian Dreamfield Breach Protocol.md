# Saint Radian Dreamfield Breach Protocol

### Internal Order Operations Framework

Saint Radian sits on a **high-resonance Dreamfield fault line**, meaning the boundary between the human world and the mythic layers is naturally thinner here.

The academy was intentionally constructed at this location to:

- study Dreamfield phenomena
    
- harvest archetypal energy
    
- train operatives capable of interacting with mythic entities
    

Because breaches are expected, the Order maintains a **five-stage containment system**.

---

# Phase 1 — Resonance Detection

### (Pre-Breach Monitoring)

Before a creature manifests, the Dreamfield produces **symbolic instability patterns**.

Saint Radian tracks these using three systems:

### Emotional Telemetry

The **Division Engine** measures emotional output across campus.

High levels of:

- rage
    
- envy
    
- humiliation
    
- fear
    

increase Dreamfield pressure.

This is why the academy encourages rivalries.

The emotional output literally **weakens the boundary layer**.

---

### Dreamer Monitoring

Students in the **Astral Chorus** are watched closely.

Dreamers like Selene act as **conduits** between layers.

Signs of instability include:

- recurring archetypal dreams
    
- prophetic imagery
    
- symbolic animals appearing in dreams
    

These signals indicate a potential breach.

---

### Artifact Sensors

Certain structures around Saint Radian contain **Dreamfield resonance glyphs**.

When the boundary destabilizes:

- sigils begin glowing
    
- the Obsidian Mirror records anomalies
    
- hidden instruments in the Order’s control chambers activate
    

---

# Phase 2 — Breach Event

### (Manifestation)

When a rupture occurs, the event typically begins with:

- environmental distortion
    
- sound pressure changes
    
- symbolic imagery appearing in the air
    
- structural damage as the creature crosses dimensions
    

The creature will then manifest physically.

The Order classifies the event as:

|Breach Level|Description|
|---|---|
|Level I|minor echo entity|
|Level II|primal beast manifestation|
|Level III|archetype avatar|

The Centaur incident is classified as **Level II — Primal Beast**.

---

# Phase 3 — Rapid Response

When a breach occurs, the academy deploys **student response teams** trained to contain Dreamfield entities.

Each role corresponds to a combat specialization.

---

### Arcane Control — Elias

Primary role:

- binding magic
    
- temporal slowing
    
- containment fields
    

Elias freezing the Centaur briefly fits perfectly into this protocol.

His job is to **stabilize the creature long enough for the rest of the team to act**.

---

### Physical Interdiction — Kaelen

Role:

- close-quarters engagement
    
- redirecting creature movement
    
- absorbing impact
    

Kaelen jumping onto the Centaur’s back represents a **standard intercept maneuver**.

He prevents the creature from charging further into populated areas.

---

### Arcane Execution — DeMarco

Role:

- destructive spellcasting
    
- lethal containment if necessary
    

When DeMarco ignites the Centaur with fire magic, he is performing **Protocol Option Gamma: lethal neutralization**.

This is why he refuses Elara’s plea.

From the Order’s perspective:

The creature is an **anomalous threat**.

---

### Empathic Analysis — Elara

Role:

- emotional resonance
    
- determining creature intent
    
- detecting sentience
    

Elara realizing the Centaur is confused rather than hostile is exactly what this role is meant to reveal.

However, the Order usually **ignores empathic findings**.

---

### Security Liaison — Grant

Grant’s role is interesting.

He acts as a **situational responder**, protecting civilians.

Saving Alexander and Spencer fits perfectly with this role.

---

# Phase 4 — Containment

After a creature is neutralized:

1. the area is evacuated
    
2. Order recovery teams arrive
    
3. the creature’s body is confiscated
    

Students are told the creature has **disintegrated**.

In reality the Order transports the remains to **restricted research laboratories**.

This explains the mutilated Centaur carcass discovered later.

---

# Phase 5 — Narrative Control

The final step is controlling the story.

The Order suppresses information using:

- Jangler enforcement
    
- rumor manipulation
    
- official statements
    

Students are told:

> “A minor Dreamfield anomaly occurred. The situation has been resolved.”

But rumors always spread.

---

# Why the Order Allows Breaches

Here’s the disturbing truth.

The Order does not simply **respond to breaches**.

They often **encourage the conditions that cause them**.

Because each manifestation provides:

- new biological samples
    
- archetypal data
    
- mythic energy signatures
    

Saint Radian is essentially a **Dreamfield research facility disguised as a school**.

---

# Where the Mystery Begins

After the Centaur battle, something strange happens.

The recovery teams never report retrieving the body.

Later the mutilated corpse appears.

Which means:

Someone **intercepted the containment process**.

This is where rumors of the **masked executioner** begin.

Students begin asking:

- Who took the Centaur?
    
- Why was it dissected?
    
- Who has the power to bypass the Order?
    

And suddenly the campus starts whispering about **a hidden judge moving through the shadows**.

Sigmatix sees something else.

He notices that the **breach occurred immediately after the Shipping Wars conflict spike**.

He writes quietly in his notes:

This becomes one of the first hints that the academy’s true purpose is far darker than anyone suspects.
