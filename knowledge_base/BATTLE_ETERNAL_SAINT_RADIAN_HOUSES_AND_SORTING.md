# OVERVIEW

Saint Radian Academy organizes students into eight primary Houses and Orders. Each society maintains its own dormitory, occult specialty, philosophical identity, ability system, apex leadership figure, color palette, signature beasts, rivalries, and alliances.

These organizations exist in a fragile equilibrium — ideological conflicts, sabotage campaigns, and power struggles shape student life and mirror the deeper war between the Order of the Black Sun and emerging resistance forces. The Division Engine harvests emotional energy from every House, turning their unique gifts into fuel for the Order's planetary control infrastructure.

Each House represents a fundamentally different approach to controlling reality. Together they form a complete Domain Architecture — eight ways reality can be manipulated, defined, or distorted.

# 1. ORDER OF THE OBSIDIAN GLYPHS — The Keepers of Inscribed Truth

**Motto:** "Truth hides in etchings."
**Dorm:** The Sepulchre
**Colors:** #0B0B0D (Obsidian Black), #2C1E3F (Deep Violet), #C8A96A (Burnished Gold), #E8E4D8 (Pale Bone)
**Focus:** Occult linguistics, etymology, sigil-crafting, narrative inscriptions
**Specialization:** Light & dark glyph systems, beast-binding scripts, ciphered communication
**Status:** Fearful, secretive, academically prestigious
**Signature Beasts:** Harpies, Centaurs, Yatagarasu
**Domain:** Meaning → Inscription

The oldest intellectual society at Saint Radian. Students believe every truth is written before it is spoken, every outcome etched in hidden inscriptions. They are custodians of symbolic reality architecture — all power, magic, and meaning originates from inscription.

**Dormitory — The Sepulchre:** Shadowy stone halls covered with etched walls glowing faintly at night. A vast dark library with floating glyph constructs and walls covered in inscriptions. Being inside feels like standing inside recorded reality.

**Occult Specialty:** Binding scripts and inscriptions that either control beasts (Dark Glyphs) or harmonize with them (Light Glyphs). Their "translations" are secretly harvested by the Division Engine.

**Uniform Accents:** Silver-stitched SR crests, cuff runes faintly glowing when students focus. Layered robes with engraved surfaces and glowing sigils etched into fabric.

### Ability System — Inscription Authority

- **Tier 1 — Glyph Activation:** Create simple sigils, reinforce barriers, store energy in symbols.
- **Tier 2 — Script Binding:** Chain glyphs into sequences, create persistent effects, bind actions to conditions.
- **Tier 3 — Reality Inscription:** Write rules into localized space, alter system behavior, override weaker constructs.
- **Legendary Tier — True Name Encoding:** Inscribe the true nature of a thing, gain control over it, rewrite its function.

### Higher Authority — The Scriptorium Synod

An ancient governing body beyond Saint Radian that preserves original glyph systems, controls access to forbidden inscriptions, and maintains symbolic canon. They decide which symbols are valid, deprecated, or forbidden entirely. They don't control magic — they control what magic is allowed to mean.

### Apex — Aurelian Voss ("The Grand Inscriber")

Aurelian Voss is the living extension of symbolic authority. He doesn't experiment, argue, or feel — he decides what things mean.

**Signature Ability — Script Imposition:** Writes glyphs into space mid-air, inscribes rules directly into reality, overwrites weaker systems instantly.

**The Living Script:** His body acts as a moving glyph system. Symbols shift across his skin and clothing. Reality adjusts around his presence.

**Canon Lock (Absolute):** Freezes a system into a fixed state. Prevents alteration or reinterpretation. Direct counter to Aetherion.

**Appearance:** Sharp, elegant features. Parchment-pale skin. Deep gold eyes (#C8A96A) with faint inner glow. Long straight jet-black hair. Layered black robes with gold inscriptions and floating glyph fragments. Silence from him feels like judgment.

# THE SORTING CEREMONY

## Episode 1 Key Scene

First major institutional ritual at Saint Radian Academy. Introduces the House system, the Obsidian Mirror, and the first anomaly in the sorting algorithm.

## Scene Purpose

The Sorting Ceremony serves multiple story functions:

- Introduces the Houses and Orders of Saint Radian
- Establishes the prestige and mystique of the academy
- Reveals the institutional control systems operating behind the scenes
- Foreshadows the manipulation of the Division Engine

Most importantly: it becomes the **first moment where the system fails to categorize Alexander correctly.**

## Setting — The Grand Atrium

The ceremony takes place in the central chamber of Saint Radian. Architectural features include towering vaulted ceilings, marble floors etched with sacred geometry, banners representing each House, and a circular platform at the center of the hall.

At the center stands **The Obsidian Mirror** — a vertical slab of polished black stone approximately three meters tall. Its surface appears reflective but slightly distorted. Students are told it is an advanced AI system. In truth, it is a **Dreamfield resonance interface**.

## The Ceremony Process

First-year students gather in the atrium. Upperclassmen watch from elevated balconies. The Headmaster explains the ritual:

> "Saint Radian does not place you into Houses. Your nature reveals where you belong."

Students are called forward one by one. Each approaches the Mirror. The room grows quiet. The Mirror begins asking questions.

## The Mirror Questions

The Mirror's voice is calm and neutral. Almost mechanical. But occasionally students report hearing something behind the voice.

Questions vary for each student. Examples:

- "What do you fear becoming?"
- "Would you betray a friend to prevent a war?"
- "Is truth more important than loyalty?"
- "Who deserves power?"

Students respond verbally. However, the system records far more than answers. It analyzes emotional responses, hesitation, subconscious reactions, and Dreamfield resonance. The Mirror produces a House designation moments later.

## House Assignments

Assignments appear on a massive holographic projection above the atrium. Students from the announced House cheer. The ritual appears celebratory. But unseen observers from the Order of the Black Sun monitor the data stream. Each assignment feeds the **Archetype Distribution Engine**.
