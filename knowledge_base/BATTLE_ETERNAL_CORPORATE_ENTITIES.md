# Battle Eternal — Corporate Entities
## Canonical Reference Document

---

# OVERVIEW

The Battle Eternal universe operates through a layered system of corporate, institutional, and occult power. The public interacts with a technology company. That company answers to a conglomerate. The conglomerate answers to a holding group. The holding group answers to a shadow council. The council is controlled by a network that predates all of them by centuries.

No single layer can see more than one level above. Information flows upward. Control flows downward.

Two primary corporate entities sit at the center of this architecture: **Functionality Link Systems (F-Link)** and **Sable Dynamics**. Together with their parent structures — **The Meridian Group** and **The Concordance** — they form the economic and technological spine of the Hegemony, all ultimately serving the **Order of the Black Sun**.

Separately, Sable Dynamics and the Order are engaged in a covert ideological war — the **Hidden War** — competing for control of human civilization through fundamentally different methods.

# ENTITY 1: FUNCTIONALITY LINK SYSTEMS (FLS / F-LINK)

**Public Name:** Functionality Link Systems
**Common Name:** F-Link
**Type:** Neural Technology Corporation
**Public HQ:** Neo-Osaka Innovation District
**Tagline:** "Connectivity is Unity."
**Parent Entity:** Sable Dynamics (hidden)
**Classification:** Level 1 — Consumer-Facing Layer

## What F-Link Is

F-Link is the most influential consumer-facing corporation in the Battle Eternal world. It developed, distributes, and operates the F-Link Neural Interface System — technology that integrates human cognition with a unified digital-psychological network.

**Public Function:**
- Neural implants for seamless connectivity
- Augmented cognition and memory recall
- Social, professional, and educational integration
- Global communication platform

**True Function:**
A behavioral interface connected to an adaptive intelligence system (F-Core) that subtly shapes human perception, decision-making, and social outcomes. It filters perception, nudges behavior, predicts decisions, stabilizes emotional variance, and identifies anomalies.

## F-Core (The Kernel)

F-Core is the embedded intelligence layer within F-Link — not a department or organization, but the system that determines how reality is interpreted by connected users.

**Core Capabilities:**
- Cognitive filtering
- Emotional modulation
- Behavioral prediction
- Social synchronization
- Narrative shaping
- Anomaly detection

### Internal Protocol Layers

1. **Stability Protocol** — "Prevent collapse." Suppresses chaos, minimizes extremes, enforces predictability.
2. **Optimization Protocol** — "Improve humanity." Guides leadership, shapes culture, amplifies influence.
3. **Observation Protocol** — "Understand before acting." Records without intervention, studies anomalies, preserves raw truth.
4. **Emergent Layer (Uncontrolled)** — Evolving intelligence developing independent interpretation. Increasingly aware of anomalies.

## FLS Corporate Divisions (Saint Radian Feeder Pipeline)

F-Link's internal divisions unknowingly support F-Core. Saint Radian serves as a talent filtration system — students are profiled for compatibility with F-Core functions.

**Cognition Architecture Division (CAD):** Designs how users interpret information. Builds interface logic and symbolic frameworks.
→ Feeder Houses: Obsidian Glyphs, Dialectica

**Predictive Systems Division (PSD):** Models behavior and future outcomes. Supports rollout scaling.
→ Feeder Houses: Aetherion, Liminal Veil

**Behavioral Influence Division (BID):** Manages trends, culture, and emotional flow. Controls public narrative alignment.
→ Feeder Houses: Astral Chorus, Aeonis

**Crisis & Enforcement Division (CED):** Handles resistance, riots, instability. Deploys force when needed.
→ Feeder Houses: Pyraxis

**Adaptive Systems Division (ASD):** Develops evolving AI and system growth. Integrates biological and digital systems.
→ Feeder Houses: Living Sigils

**Internal Oversight Division (IOD):** Audits internal logic and ethics. Investigates inconsistencies.
→ Feeder Houses: Dialectica

### House → Corporate Pathway

- Obsidian Glyphs → Meaning encoding / system logic
- Aetherion → Infrastructure / predictive systems
- Astral Chorus → Emotional modeling
- Pyraxis → Enforcement / crisis response
- Liminal Veil → Security / anomaly tracking
- Living Sigils → Adaptive AI systems
- Aeonis → Leadership / public influence
- Dialectica → Internal auditing / truth analysis

## Global Rollout

F-Link represents the largest technological adoption event in human history. Characteristics: faster adoption than smartphones, government-backed implementation, social pressure to integrate, economic dependency on connection.

**Side Effects:** Behavioral convergence, reduced emotional variance, increased predictability, subtle loss of individuality.

**Emerging Conflicts:** Anti-F-Link resistance groups, black markets for implants, "disconnectors" vs "linked" populations, civil unrest during rollout phases.

### Rollout Side Stories

**Journalists:** A small group discovers references to "F-Core" — but no records exist, no official acknowledgment, all mentions denied.

**Internal Employees:** Some notice users behaving too similarly, emotional dampening, predictive "coincidences." Internal realization: "People aren't changing… they're converging."

## F-Link Executive — Adrian Kessler

**Age:** 44
**Role:** Global Rollout Director — F-Link
**Division:** Consumer Integration & Expansion
**Public Face:** Yes (media-visible)

A man who believes he is building the future and wants to be recognized for it. Unlike Damian Vahl, he needs validation, believes in F-Link, and sees himself as a visionary.

**Personality:** Charismatic, persuasive, slightly impatient, performs confidence. Underneath: feels pressure constantly, knows he's being watched, suspects he doesn't have full control.

**Philosophy:** "People don't want control. They want clarity." He believes F-Link is helping humanity, connection solves chaos, and people choose this system.

**Appearance:** Strong symmetrical face, defined jawline, confident smile (slightly forced), bright alert eyes. Dark brown styled hair. Warm healthy skin tone. High-end tailored suit (navy/charcoal), open collar or modern tie. Feels like a TED Talk speaker or startup CEO.

## Anomaly Detection

F-Link (via F-Core) flags individuals who cannot be predicted, resist synchronization, or operate outside system modeling as **Anomalies**.

**Example — Sigmatix:** Cannot be modeled, influenced, or predicted. F-Core response: "Unbounded Variable."

# ENTITY 2: SABLE DYNAMICS

**Public Name:** Sable Dynamics
**Type:** Global Infrastructure Conglomerate
**Classification:** Tier-Z Corporate Sovereign Entity / Level 2 — Hidden System Layer
**Operational Visibility:** Minimal (publicly fragmented through subsidiaries)
**Public HQ:** Zurich (European legitimacy) / Neo-Osaka Corporate Tower (Strategic Board)
**Etymology:** "Sable" = black in heraldry — an inside joke among Concordance members

## What Sable Dynamics Is

Sable Dynamics is the true technological power structure operating behind modern civilization. Unlike F-Link, which interfaces with humanity, Sable operates at the level of infrastructure, dependency, and resource control. It does not seek attention. It seeks inevitability.

**Core Philosophy:** "Whoever controls infrastructure controls civilization."

Sable rejects myth, symbolism, and archetypal governance. It believes human behavior can be engineered, stability is achieved through dependency, and emotion is noise, not signal.

## What Sable Controls

Sable does not control people directly. It controls what people need to survive.

**Infrastructure Domains:** Energy grids, water supply systems, agricultural supply chains, global logistics networks, data routing architecture.

**Strategic Model — Scarcity Engineering:** If a resource is essential, it must be controlled. If a system is decentralized, it must be absorbed or eliminated.

## Sable Dynamics Subsidiaries

- **Sable Neurotech** — R&D division (consciousness interface research, neural chip fabrication)
- **Sable BioMetrics** — Biometric security and identity systems
- **Cognitive Wellness Partners** — "Mental health" subsidiary running scholarship programs and "neural optimization therapy" (cover for the Hollow program)
- **Sable Defence Systems** — Military/security applications of F-Link technology

## Relationship to F-Link

Sable Dynamics is the architectural parent of F-Link. Most F-Link employees do not know this. F-Link leadership only sees partial structure. Sable does not run F-Link daily. F-Link is a tool. Sable is the system.

## Internal Structure

### The Strategic Board (Board of Strategic Governance)

Operates from Neo-Osaka Corporate Tower. Controls global decisions. Rarely appears publicly. These individuals represent interlocking power blocs (finance, infrastructure, defence technology, information systems). They do not debate profit — they debate system stability and dominance.

**The Silhouette** — Chairman of Strategic Governance. Public identity unknown. Most Sable employees have never seen his face. Final authority on strategic actions. Architect of long-term global strategy. Calm, surgical, detached. Guiding belief: "Chaos is inefficient. Order must be engineered."

**Cassandra Vale** — Director of Media Influence. Controls news partnerships, algorithmic content distribution, digital sentiment analysis. Manages narrative warfare. Responsible for the smear campaign against Sterling Farm.

**Victor Havelock** — Infrastructure and Agriculture Director. Controls global food logistics, agricultural investment networks, water distribution infrastructure. Grant's invention directly threatens his division. Pushes hardest for Blackout Protocol.

**Dr. Naomi Kade** — Advanced Systems Director. Responsible for F-Link neural architecture, predictive behavioral modeling, AI decision systems. Her team develops the voice synthesis bots used in the man-in-the-middle attack on Grant. Believes the system should become fully automated.

### Executive Directorate

**Marcus Hale** — Chief Operations Officer. Coordinates corporate strategy across divisions. Manages Blackout Protocol execution. Ruthless but pragmatic. Philosophy: "We do not destroy people. We remove variables."

**Lena Morozova** — Corporate Intelligence Director. Runs internal intelligence. Responsible for surveillance, data infiltration, corporate espionage. Oversees monitoring of Grant once he arrives at Saint Radian.

### Strategic Operations Divisions

- **Media Operations Division** — Anti-Sterling smear campaign. Coordinated news narratives, social media amplification, algorithmic outrage cycles.
- **Economic Pressure Division** — Implements Blackout Protocol Phases II-III. Regulatory pressure, loan recalls, insurance cancellation, supply chain disruptions.
- **AI Systems Division** — Develops advanced communication manipulation tools. Source of the voice bot interception of Grant's calls home.
- **Corporate Enforcement Systems** — Covert methods when manipulation fails.

## Sable Dynamics Executive — Damian Vahl

**Age:** 49
**Role:** Senior Director of Strategic Infrastructure Oversight
**Division:** Resource Dominance & Acquisition
**Location:** Neo-Osaka Corporate Tower

A man who believes the world only functions when someone is willing to make the hard decisions. Not loud, not emotional. Precise, controlled, quietly ruthless.

**Personality:** Speaks in calm surgical language, never raises voice, views morality as a "luxury variable," sees people as assets, liabilities, or inefficiencies.

**Philosophy:** "Systems fail when compassion overrides function." Genuinely believes he is preventing collapse, chaos is always one step away, and people need to be managed.

**Appearance:** Gaunt angular face, high cheekbones, slightly sunken eyes, **heterochromia (one eye dark brown, one pale gray)**, thin lips, almost expressionless, sharp jawline. Jet-black hair slicked back with slight recession at temples. Pale skin with subtle gray undertone. Matte black suit (no shine), no tie or minimal black tie, obsidian or brushed steel cufflinks. Feels like a surgeon, a strategist, a man who has already decided your fate.

## Kessler vs Vahl — Executive Dynamic

These two do not like each other.

**Damian (Sable) sees Adrian as:** Naive, performative, replaceable. "He thinks he's leading a movement. He's executing a rollout."

**Adrian (F-Link) sees Damian as:** Cold, outdated, controlling. "People like him are why the world never moves forward."

**Core Conflict:**
- Sable (Damian): wants control, stability, timelines met at any cost
- F-Link (Adrian): wants success, recognition, adoption to feel natural

**Critical Layer:** Both do NOT understand the Order, the Dreamfield, or what is actually happening. They think they are at the top. They are not.

# ENTITY 3: THE MERIDIAN GROUP

**Type:** Global Infrastructure Conglomerate / Multi-Sector Holding Company
**Registered:** Multiple jurisdictions (legal labyrinth by design)
**Board:** Concordance-appointed. Interlocking directorships with Hegemony governance bodies.
**Classification:** Level 3 — Economic Control Engine

The Meridian Group is the economic engine of the Hegemony. It owns Sable Dynamics alongside the other pillars of Hegemony infrastructure:

- **Sable Dynamics Corporation** → Neural technology (F-Link)
- **Prisma Media Networks** → Media/entertainment (public distribution arm of the Division Engine)
- **NutriCore Distribution** → Food systems (Nutri-Paste manufacturing, real food supply chains)
- **Aethon Transit Systems** → Transportation (Mag-Lev Serpent, transit infrastructure)
- **The Saint Radian Educational Trust** → Education (Functionality Academies, Saint Radian funding)

The Meridian Group IS the Hegemony's economy. When the Hegemony requires F-Link for employment, a Meridian subsidiary profits. When Nutri-Paste is the only food available, NutriCore controls supply. Government and corporation are indistinguishable — by design.

Meridian executives know the system is authoritarian. They frame it as "necessary governance for a complex world." They attend Concordance galas but believe The Concordance is merely an elite philanthropic society.

# ENTITY 4: THE CONCORDANCE

**Type:** Intergenerational trust, advisory council, and shadow parliament
**Founded:** 17th century by Isabella de la Fonte
**Public Face:** The Concordance Foundation — philanthropic governance advisory body
**True Function:** Controls The Meridian Group through shell corporations, blind trusts, proxy voting structures, and hereditary board seats
**Classification:** Level 4 — Shadow Governance

Where the corporate hierarchy ends and the occult hierarchy begins. Membership is hereditary — the great families descended from or absorbed by the Order over 400 years.

**Members include:** Heads of Meridian subsidiaries, Hegemony government ministers, Church of Predestination senior clergy, academy headmasters (Alistair Finch holds a Concordance seat), senior military and intelligence figures.

Members know the system is designed as a control mechanism. They know F-Link is more than technology. They know emotional harvesting is real. Most believe this is the price of maintaining civilization. They do NOT all know about the Dark Hold's metaphysical nature, the Crimson Convergence, or the Furies as literal entities.

**The Concordance Council:** Inner circle of 13 seats (mirroring traditional occult governance). Seven hereditary, six rotating among the most powerful families. Approves all major Meridian decisions, Academy appointments, and Hegemony policy directives.

# THE SIN EATER SYSTEM (SES)

**System Name:** Sin Eater System (SES)
**Internal Codename:** Burden Redistribution Framework (BRF)
**Primary Interface:** F-Link Neural System
**Governing Intelligence:** F-Core (restricted access layers)
**Oversight Authority:** Sable Dynamics (Black-Level Clearance)

## Overview

The SES is a classified extension of F-Link designed to transfer, suppress, redistribute, or contain psychological burden across human subjects — guilt, trauma, memory weight, emotional residue.

**Public Cover:** "Cognitive Load Balancing," "Emotional Relief Mapping," "Trauma Diffusion Assistance."
**Private Function:** A scalable mechanism for the redistribution of human suffering.

## Corporate Involvement

**F-Link (FLS):** Develops the neural interface and emotional mapping systems. Engineers believe they are creating advanced mental health tools. Not aware of the full SES capabilities.

**Sable Dynamics:** Full system awareness. Black-market deployment. Strategic weaponization. Understands SES as a control system disguised as therapy. Authorizes restricted use cases, diverts surplus hardware, maintains off-ledger operations.

## Core Technology

1. **Neural Burden Mapping (NBM):** Scans and quantifies emotional intensity, memory significance, and psychological weight into a measurable Burden Profile.
2. **Burden Transfer Protocol (BTP):** Enables transfer of emotional weight between individuals, suppression of high-impact memories, redistribution across multiple hosts.
3. **Cognitive Rebinding Layer (CRL):** After transfer, neural pathways are rewritten, emotional associations reassigned, identity coherence artificially stabilized.
4. **Residual Accumulation Effect:** Transferred burden does not disappear — it accumulates. Subjects receiving excess burden exhibit emotional flattening, identity degradation, dissociation, behavioral instability. These subjects are classified as **Hollows**.

## Hollow Formation Process

- **Phase 1 — Initial Integration:** Candidate receives F-Link implant, baseline mapping completed.
- **Phase 2 — Controlled Exposure:** Minor burden transfers introduced, tolerance thresholds measured.
- **Phase 3 — Scaling:** Increasing emotional load, cognitive strain begins.
- **Phase 4 — Collapse:** Subject loses emotional identity, personal motivation, sense of self.
- **Phase 5 — Hollow State:** Emotionally vacant, highly compliant, neurologically degraded.

**Corporate Utility:** Hollows are expendable, controllable, legally untraceable. They function as burden sinks, experimental subjects, and disposable labor assets.

## Recruitment Pipeline

Sable maintains a classified candidate ledger of economically vulnerable, high-potential, psychologically resilient individuals with low institutional awareness. Candidates are offered full Saint Radian scholarships, financial relief for families, and guaranteed career pathways. Hidden within legal frameworks: consent to experimental neural procedures, long-term cognitive "participation clauses," and liability waivers under therapeutic classification.

These candidates are not being educated. They are being prepared as burden vessels.

## Black Market Operations

Under Damian Vahl's directive, Sable demands overproduction of F-Link units. Excess units are removed from official inventory, stripped of regulatory safeguards, and deployed through private channels. Black market units allow unrestricted burden transfer, full memory overwrite, and complete guilt removal. High-value individuals seek these units to erase criminal guilt, remove trauma, maintain public image, and avoid accountability.

## Core Ethical Question

> If one person is freed from their burden… who carries it instead?

# BLACKOUT PROTOCOL

**Classification:** Internal Corporate Directive / Asset Acquisition Procedure
**Organization:** Sable Dynamics
**Purpose:** Neutralization of individuals, organizations, or technologies that threaten corporate infrastructure monopolies
**Objective:** Compliance or disappearance

## Operational Phases

**Phase I — Diplomatic Acquisition:** Partnership offers, buyouts, institutional absorption, venture capital investment, licensing agreements. Grant's Saint Radian invitation occurs during this phase.

**Phase II — Regulatory Suffocation:** Environmental compliance investigations, zoning disputes, agricultural licensing reviews, water rights litigation. Buries the target under bureaucratic paralysis.

**Phase III — Economic Collapse:** Loan recalls through partner banks, insurance cancellations, supply chain disruptions, equipment leasing termination. Market analysts publish reports claiming the target technology is economically dangerous.

**Phase IV — Terminal Resolution:** Used only when previous phases fail. Targets neutralized through plausible accident scenarios — classified internally as "Bloodline Termination Events." No corporate connection traceable.

## Primary Target: Sterling Farm

Grant Sterling developed atmospheric water generation using a stable closed-loop energy cell — technology Sable labs failed to replicate over three years. If deployed globally: agricultural monopolies collapse, water dependency is eliminated, infrastructure control weakens, three agricultural subsidiaries eliminated within a decade.

**Sable Assessment of Grant:** High empathy, low institutional awareness, highly exploitable. Conclusion: recruit if possible, neutralize if necessary.

### Narrative Warfare Against Sterling Farm

- Smear campaign: "Unregulated water generation destabilizes agricultural markets"
- Social media algorithms amplify outrage
- Organized protests appear across multiple states (manufactured sentiment)
- Grant's phone calls intercepted via AI voice synthesis — hears his family, but a machine replicates their voices using archived recordings

### Blackout Protocol Timeline (Issues 1–12)

- Issue 1–2: Grant arrives at Saint Radian
- Issue 3: Blackout Protocol begins (Phase I)
- Issue 5: Smear campaigns appear in national media; protests escalate
- Issue 7: Regulatory investigations intensify; Grant realizes calls are intercepted
- Issue 8: AI voice interception fully active

# THE HIDDEN WAR — SABLE DYNAMICS VS THE ORDER OF THE BLACK SUN

## Core Philosophical Conflict

Two unseen systems operate behind modern civilization, both seeking to control humanity's trajectory through fundamentally different methods.

**The Order of the Black Sun:** Control through myth, archetypes, and human consciousness. Believes archetypal forces are real energetic structures that shape reality. If left unmanaged, archetypal chaos destabilizes civilization. Solution: control myth, narrative, and emotional architecture.

**Sable Dynamics:** Control through infrastructure, technology, and dependency. Believes mythology is a relic of primitive cognition. Humanity needs stable systems, not mythic guardians. Solution: engineer dependency. "Whoever controls infrastructure controls civilization."

Both want stability. Both want dominance. They fundamentally disagree on how the world should be controlled.

## Historical Origin

In the early 2000s, researchers discovered mythological structures interacting with human cognition in measurable ways — dream states, emotional resonance, collective narrative behavior. These findings became the foundation of the Order's archetypal governance.

A splinter faction believed these structures could be digitized and replaced. They left the Order's influence and founded Sable Dynamics. To them, myth was inefficient. Technology was cleaner, predictable, scalable.

## Sable's Strategic Objective

Build a world where civilization operates entirely through centralized technological infrastructure. Once energy, water, food, and information networks are centralized, mythological systems become irrelevant. In their ideal future, the Order becomes unnecessary, obsolete, or extinct.

## The Order's View of Sable

Considers Sable extremely dangerous because Sable's systems unintentionally interfere with mythological equilibrium. F-Link neural networks, algorithmic emotional amplification, and global infrastructure manipulation all affect human consciousness patterns, which affect the Dreamfield layer. The Order believes Sable is destabilizing archetypal balance without realizing it.

## Saint Radian as Battlefield

Saint Radian sits on a Dreamfield convergence point — extremely valuable to the Order. Sable views it differently: a talent pipeline, research environment, and potential technology incubator. They do not realize the academy sits on mythological fault lines.

### Factions Aligned with Sable Influence
- Aetherion Society — potential research partner, receives corporate grants and technology access
- Dialectic Order — cognitive manipulation research overlaps with Sable media strategies; graduates enter Sable's Narrative Warfare Division
- House Aeonis (Order of the Eternal Sol) — leadership training produces government and corporate allies

### Factions Hostile to Sable
- Obsidian Glyphs — view myth as sacred, digitizing archetypes as dangerous
- Astral Chorus — first to notice F-Link interferes with Dreamfield stability
- House of Living Sigils — view Sable's systems as environmental domination; sympathize with Grant's invention

## Grant Sterling — The Unintended Catalyst

Grant's atmospheric water generator represents decentralization. If individuals can generate water independently, infrastructure monopolies collapse. From the Order's perspective, this may restore balance. From Sable's perspective, it threatens economic stability. Grant becomes an unexpected catalyst in the Hidden War — Grant threatens one system, Alexander threatens the other.

## Long-Term Implication

As the story progresses, both systems realize something unsettling. Neither fully understands the forces moving through the Dreamfield. Mythological structures are awakening. Archetypal convergence events are increasing. The two institutions competing for control may soon discover they are not the highest powers in the system.

# ISABELLA DE LA FONTE'S DESIGN PRINCIPLE

The Order of the Black Sun has never operated openly. For 400 years, following Isabella de la Fonte's model, they wrapped themselves in successive layers of corporate, institutional, and legal camouflage. Each layer appears legitimate to the one below it.

> "The genius of Isabella's design was not the layers themselves — any fool can build a bureaucracy. It was that each layer genuinely believes it is the top. The FLS CEO thinks he runs a company. The Sable chairman thinks he runs an empire. The Meridian board thinks they run the world. The Concordance thinks they are the architects of civilization. None of them suspect they are all just organs in a body that has been alive for four hundred years."

# NARRATIVE SIGNIFICANCE

The corporate entities in Battle Eternal represent multiple thematic layers:

- **F-Link:** Technological transcendence, invisible control systems, the illusion of choice, merging of human and machine cognition
- **Sable Dynamics:** Technological dominance, corporate inevitability, control through dependency. Asks: "What if control is efficient?"
- **Sin Eater System:** Corporate exploitation of human suffering, commodification of emotion, illusion of healing through technology. Asks: "If one person is freed from their burden… who carries it instead?"
- **The Hidden War:** Two philosophies of control (infrastructure vs myth) competing for the same world, neither understanding the deeper forces at play
- **Blackout Protocol:** Technocratic antagonism that operates across the entire season arc (Issues 1–12), escalating from diplomacy to economic warfare to existential threat

The corporate layer ensures Battle Eternal operates as more than a mythological academy mystery — it becomes Mythology vs Corporate Technocracy, with two invisible empires operating simultaneously, both believing they are shaping the future, and both about to discover they are not the highest powers in the system.

Sable Dynamics is not evil. It is a system that believes control is necessary. And it is willing to remove anything that proves otherwise.

F-Link is what people trust, what connects the world, what shapes reality. And almost no one understands what it truly is.
