# Issue 1 — "The Axiom Trial"

## Theme
Identity vs Control

## Primary Functions
- Introduce Saint Radian
- Run the Sorting Ceremony
- Establish Houses and rivalries
- Begin Shipping Wars
- Introduce Janglers
- Begin Division Engine emotional harvesting

## Nine-Scene Structure

**Scene 1 — Arrival at Saint Radian:** Alexander and Spencer arrive at the gothic-futurist campus. Spencer documents everything. DeMarco arrives with an entourage. Crystalis is recognized by students. Shipping Wars seeds begin as students whisper about sorting placements.

**Scene 2 — Orientation & Institutional Myth:** Headmaster Finch delivers the founding myth: "Saint Radian does not assign identity. It reveals it." Hidden Order of the Black Sun observers monitor the ceremony. The sorting system actually exists to manufacture emotional conflict.

**Scene 3 — The Sorting Ceremony:** The Obsidian Mirror (a Dreamfield resonance interface disguised as AI) sorts students into Houses. Crowds cheer as faction identities form.

**Scene 4 — Alexander's Anomaly:** The Mirror asks Alexander what he wants. He answers "Freedom." The system fails — multiple archetypes detected (Leader, Disruptor, Judge, Catalyst). System forces a result: Order of the Eternal Sol. Monitoring system flags: ANOMALY DETECTED / ARCHETYPE MATRIX UNSTABLE. The Order observers notice immediately.

**Scene 5 — Houses Form & Rivalries Begin:** Engineered rivalries activate: Eternal Sol vs Obsidian Glyphs (leadership vs scholarship), Crimson Flame vs Astral Chorus (aggression vs empathy), Aetherion vs Living Sigils (tech vs biology). Division Engine quietly activates.

**Scene 6 — The Shipping Wars Ignite:** Campus gossip networks spread romantic rumors (Crystalis × DeMarco, Alexander × Crystalis, Selene × Castor). The academy encourages romantic rivalries because they produce intense emotional output — the Division Engine calibration phase.

**Scene 7 — Janglers Introduced:** Campus officials with dark uniforms and metal bracelets emitting soft chimes arrive to "enforce order." Their bracelets emit psycho-acoustic signals that increase emotional agitation. After they leave, conflicts become worse. They are emotional thermostats.

**Scene 8 — Dreamfield Instability:** Selene Anastas has her first nightmare (forest, hooves, shadowed creature). The emotional spike weakened the Dreamfield boundary, setting up the Centaur manifestation in Issue 2.

**Scene 9 — Hidden Layer (Order Perspective):** Order analysts in a hidden control room review sorting data on Alexander (ARCHETYPE CLUSTER COLLISION / POSSIBLE SOURCE VESSEL). Division Engine output increased 18% since sorting. Final shot: Janglers walking through campus corridors, bracelets still ringing.

1. Social Trigger (Sorting Ceremony → Faction Identity → House Rivalries)

# Issue 2 — "The Centaur Manifestation"

## Five-Phase Scene Structure

**Phase 1 — The Rupture:** Saint Radian corridor. Deep impact sound. Wall explodes inward. The Centaur enters — shoulders scraping the ceiling, hooves cracking marble, breath fogging the air. Disoriented but powerful.

**Phase 2 — Panic:** Chaos erupts. The Centaur bolts forward at shocking speed — faster than humans. A student is caught and killed. Another is crushed through a desk. Establishes: no one is safe.

**Phase 3 — Alexander Escape Sequence:** Alexander grabs Spencer. Chase through the corridor. The Centaur is gaining. Spencer stumbles. Grant tackles Alexander to the floor at the last second. The Centaur crashes past them.

**Phase 4 — Combat Intervention:** Elite fighters arrive. Elias raises arcane glyphs and briefly freezes the Centaur in slowed time. Kaelen drops from the upper balcony onto the Centaur's back with a bone-cracking impact. DeMarco walks forward calmly — runic symbols spiral from his wrists, chants in ancient language, snaps his fingers — the Centaur erupts in white fire.

**Phase 5 — Elara's Revelation:** Elara collapses, feeling the Centaur's mind through empathy. The Centaur doesn't understand where it is — its home was a vast forest, the air burns its lungs, gravity feels wrong. It believes it has offended a god and is trying to escape. Elara screams "Stop! It doesn't understand!" DeMarco ignores her. Kaelen intervenes with a shockwave, extinguishing flames: "That's enough." The Centaur collapses, barely alive.

## What This Scene Establishes
1. Mythic Threat — Dreamfield creatures are not safe or controllable
2. Combat Hierarchy — Elias (arcane control), Kaelen (physical combat), DeMarco (destructive magic)
3. Moral Conflict — Dreamfield entities are beings displaced from their world, not monsters
4. The Academy's Secret — The mutilated carcass appears later; someone took it

# Dreamfield Creature Classification System

Saint Radian Research Taxonomy for categorizing Dreamfield entities that breach into the Surface World.

## Class I — Echo Beasts (Weak Manifestations)
Form when emotional spikes, collective fear, or dream instability create temporary manifestations. Unstable physical form, fade after short duration, limited intelligence, symbolic behavior. Examples: shadow wolves, nightmare insects, phantom birds. Narrative role: environmental hazards, not major threats.

## Class II — Primal Beasts (The Centaur Category)
True Primal Wilds entities — conscious beings pulled across the Dreamfield boundary. Extremely powerful, highly intelligent, symbolic consciousness, hostile when displaced. NOT evil — they are out of place. Examples: Centaurs (instinct/dual nature), Ceryneian Hind (impossible pursuit), Kitsune (adaptive intelligence), Phoenix/Hō-ō (rebirth archetype).

## Class III — Archetype Avatars (Rare Entities)
Fully conscious mythic intelligences — representatives of archetypal forces. Usually do not enter the Surface World directly; interact through dreams or chosen vessels. Examples: Cerberus (guardian archetype), Hō-ō (phoenix archetype), Leviathan-class sea entities, Titan consciousness fragments.

## Why the Centaur Appears
Emotional overload + Selene's uncontrolled dream broadcasting + Division Engine amplification = Dreamfield rupture pulling a Class II Primal Beast from the Verdant Expanse into the physical world.

## What Saint Radian Believes (Public vs Private)
- Public: "Dreamfield entities are dangerous anomalies."
- Private: The Order considers these creatures resources — research goals include harvesting Dreamfield energy, extracting archetypal data, weaponizing mythic biology, studying archetype consciousness.

# The Primal Wilds Ecosystem Map

The Primal Wilds exist beyond the Dreamfield boundary — a mythic ecological layer structured around symbolic ecosystems rather than geography.

## Structure
Human World → Dreamfield (symbolic boundary) → Primal Wilds (archetypal ecosystem) → Mythic Strata (pure archetypal consciousness)

## Six Major Biomes

**1. The Verdant Expanse** (Nature / Instinct / Harmony) — Endless mythic forests. Creatures: Centaurs, Satyrs, Dryads, Great stags, Ceryneian Hind. Territorial but not malicious; panic when pulled into human world. The Issue 2 Centaur comes from here.

**2. The Ember Steppes** (War / Fury / Strength) — Volcanic plain constantly reshaped by battle. Creatures: Minotaurs, Fire drakes, Hellhounds, Chimera variants. Much more aggressive when manifested.

**3. The Moonlit Canopy** (Illusion / Trickery / Transformation) — Perpetual twilight. Creatures: Kitsune, Tanuki, Shadow lynxes, Dream serpents. Manipulate perception and identity; encounters involve illusions and deception.

**4. The Crystal Abyss** (Knowledge / Ancient Memory) — Subterranean realm of glowing crystals. Creatures: Basilisks, Oracle serpents, Crystal spiders, Memory wraiths. May contain ancient mythic data — Saint Radian researchers are extremely interested.

**5. The Storm Crown** (Divine Authority / Cosmic Order) — Floating sky realms. Creatures: Phoenix/Hō-ō, Thunderbirds, Griffins, Seraphic beasts. Extremely rare in breaches; appearance indicates major cosmic imbalance.

**6. The Veiled Depths** (Fear / Chaos / Abyss) — Dark ocean-like biome. Creatures: Leviathan fragments, Nightmare kraken, Abyssal watchers, Dream parasites. Breaches produce psychological horror events.

## Breach Conditions
Three conditions must align: Dreamfield instability + Archetypal resonance + Human emotional amplification. Saint Radian is a hotspot because it constantly produces high emotional output.

# Sable Dynamics Corporate Hierarchy

Sable Dynamics is the primary technological and infrastructure conglomerate across the Western Region. Publicly known for renewable energy, agricultural tech, F-Link neural systems, and global logistics. Privately operates as a policy-shaping technocracy. Philosophy: "Control the systems people depend on, and you control the world."

## Power Structure
Board of Strategic Governance → Executive Directorate → Strategic Operations Divisions → Corporate Enforcement Systems

## Board of Strategic Governance
- **The Silhouette** — Chairman. Identity unknown. Final authority on strategic actions; architect of long-term global strategy. "Chaos is inefficient. Order must be engineered."
- **Cassandra Vale** — Director of Media Influence. Controls news partnerships, algorithmic content distribution, digital sentiment analysis. Responsible for the anti-Sterling Farm smear campaign.
- **Victor Havelock** — Infrastructure and Agriculture Director. Controls global food logistics, agricultural investment, water distribution. Grant's invention directly threatens his division. Pushes hardest for Blackout Protocol.
- **Dr. Naomi Kade** — Advanced Systems Director. Responsible for F-Link neural architecture, predictive behavioral modeling, AI decision systems. Develops voice synthesis bots used in the man-in-the-middle attack on Grant.

## Executive Directorate
- **Marcus Hale** — COO. Coordinates Blackout Protocol execution. "We do not destroy people. We remove variables."
- **Lena Morozova** — Corporate Intelligence Director. Runs surveillance, data infiltration, corporate espionage. Oversees Grant monitoring at Saint Radian.

## Strategic Operations Divisions
- **Media Operations Division** — Executes smear campaigns via coordinated news, social media amplification, algorithmic outrage cycles.
- **Economic Pressure Division** — Implements Blackout Protocol Phases II–III via regulatory pressure, loan recalls, insurance cancellation, supply chain disruptions.
- **AI Systems Division** — Develops voice bot intercept system. When Grant calls home, his calls are intercepted and mimicked using real recordings.

## Blackout Protocol (4 Phases)
- Phase I — Diplomatic Acquisition: voluntary partnership/purchase offers. Grant's Saint Radian invitation occurs here.
- Phase II — Regulatory Suffocation: environmental compliance investigations, zoning disputes, licensing reviews, water rights litigation.
- Phase III — Economic Collapse: loan recalls, insurance cancellations, supply chain disruptions, negative market analyst reports.
- Phase IV — Terminal Resolution (Bloodline Termination): plausible accident scenarios. Classified internally as "Bloodline Termination Events."

- Issue 6: Protests emerge near Sterling Farm

## Why Grant Is Targeted
Grant's atmospheric water generator threatens three industries: agricultural water infrastructure, global food dependency chains, and energy grid stabilization. If farmers can generate water independently, entire corporate supply networks collapse.

# The Hidden War — Sable Dynamics vs The Order of the Black Sun

## Core Philosophical Conflict
- **Order of the Black Sun:** Control through myth, archetypes, and human consciousness. Believes archetypal balance maintains civilization.
- **Sable Dynamics:** Control through infrastructure, technology, and dependency. Believes mythology is a relic; technology is cleaner, predictable, scalable.

## Historical Origin
Early 2000s researchers discovered mythological structures interact measurably with human cognition. One faction pursued archetypal governance (became the Order). A splinter faction believed these structures could be digitized and replaced (became Sable Dynamics).

## Saint Radian Faction Alignments
**Sable-sympathetic:** Aetherion Society (receives corporate grants/internships), Dialectic Order (members enter Sable's Narrative Warfare Division), Eternal Sol (graduates become policy allies).
**Sable-hostile:** Obsidian Glyphs (myth is sacred), Astral Chorus (F-Link destabilizes Dreamfield), Living Sigils (Sable = ecological domination).

## Long-Term Implication
Neither Sable nor the Order fully understands the forces awakening through the Dreamfield. Archetypal convergence is increasing. Both institutions may discover they are not the highest powers in the system.

# Saint Radian Dreamfield Breach Protocol

Saint Radian sits on a high-resonance Dreamfield fault line. The academy was intentionally constructed here to study phenomena, harvest archetypal energy, and train operatives.

## Five-Phase Containment System

**Phase 1 — Resonance Detection:** Three monitoring systems — Emotional Telemetry (Division Engine measures campus emotional output), Dreamer Monitoring (Astral Chorus students like Selene act as conduits), Artifact Sensors (Dreamfield resonance glyphs glow when boundary destabilizes).

**Phase 2 — Breach Event:** Environmental distortion, sound pressure changes, symbolic imagery, structural damage. Classified as Level I (echo entity), Level II (primal beast), or Level III (archetype avatar). The Centaur incident = Level II.

**Phase 3 — Rapid Response:** Student response teams with specialized roles — Arcane Control/Elias (binding, temporal slowing, containment), Physical Interdiction/Kaelen (close-quarters, redirecting movement), Arcane Execution/DeMarco (destructive spellcasting, lethal containment), Empathic Analysis/Elara (emotional resonance, sentience detection), Security Liaison/Grant (civilian protection).

**Phase 4 — Containment:** Area evacuated, Order recovery teams confiscate the creature's body. Students told it "disintegrated." Actually transported to restricted research laboratories.

**Phase 5 — Narrative Control:** Information suppressed via Jangler enforcement, rumor manipulation, official statements. "A minor Dreamfield anomaly occurred. The situation has been resolved."

## Why the Order Allows Breaches
The Order often encourages the conditions that cause breaches because each manifestation provides new biological samples, archetypal data, and mythic energy signatures. Saint Radian is a Dreamfield research facility disguised as a school.
