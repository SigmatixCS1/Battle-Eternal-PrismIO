# Saint Radian Power Hierarchy

### Hidden Governance Structure

From the outside, Saint Radian appears to be a prestigious academy.

Internally it functions as a **controlled mythological research and containment facility**.

The true chain of authority looks like this:

```
Mythic Oversight Layer
    ↓
Order Governance Layer
    ↓
Institutional Operations Layer
    ↓
Student Ecosystem
```

---

# Layer 1 — Mythic Oversight

### The Furies

The Furies are not administrators.

They are **cosmic regulators of emotional and moral imbalance**.

Their interest in Saint Radian is simple:

Saint Radian produces massive emotional output.

That output feeds larger mythological systems.

They rarely intervene directly.

Instead they influence events through **agents and pressure on the Order**.

---

# Layer 2 — Order Governance

This is where **Casey and Thorne enter the system**.

These individuals operate within the **Order of the Black Sun**, the hidden authority controlling Saint Radian.

---

## Alistair Finch

**Position:** Headmaster of Saint Radian

Public role:

- academic leader
    
- mentor to elite students
    

Hidden role:

- administrator of Dreamfield research
    
- manager of the Division Engine
    
- liaison between the academy and the Order
    

Finch believes strongly in **maintaining control of the system**.

---

## Casey

**Position:** Strategic Director for Order Operations

Casey handles:

- long-term planning
    
- political strategy
    
- narrative control
    

Where Finch manages the academy day-to-day, Casey manages **the larger agenda**.

Personality:

- calm
    
- analytical
    
- ruthless when necessary
    

Casey views Saint Radian as **a strategic asset**, not a school.

---

## Thorne

**Position:** Field Operations Commander

Thorne oversees:

- containment teams
    
- breach response protocols
    
- Order security forces
    

If something goes wrong at Saint Radian, Thorne is the one responsible for **fixing it**.

Personality:

- blunt
    
- pragmatic
    
- deeply suspicious of instability
    

---

# Layer 3 — Institutional Operations

Below them operate the Houses, Orders, and Societies.

These factions are simultaneously:

- educational groups
    
- research divisions
    
- political factions competing for influence
    

Their rivalries generate **the emotional pressure the system requires**.

---

# Layer 4 — Student Ecosystem

At the bottom of the structure are the students.

They believe they are competing for prestige.

In reality they are **participants in a mythological experiment**.

---

# After the Centaur Incident

## Order Command Meeting

Location: Hidden chamber beneath Saint Radian.

A circular room illuminated by floating sigils.

The Centaur battle has just ended.

A holographic projection shows the aftermath: shattered hallways, injured students.

---

### Dialogue Scene

Finch stands with his hands behind his back.

The projection flickers.

Casey studies the footage carefully.

Thorne leans against the wall, arms crossed.

---

**Thorne**

“Level II breach. Inside the academic wing.”

He shakes his head.

“That should not have been possible.”

---

**Finch**

“It was contained.”

---

Thorne gestures toward the image of the burning Centaur.

---

**Thorne**

“Contained after half the hallway was destroyed.”

“Three critical injuries.”

“Two unresponsive.”

He turns toward Finch.

“You call that contained?”

---

Casey interrupts calmly.

---

**Casey**

“The more interesting question is why it happened.”

Casey zooms the projection to the moment the Centaur first appeared.

Energy readings flicker across the display.

---

**Casey**

“Dreamfield resonance spiked immediately following the student conflict event.”

---

Finch sighs.

---

**Finch**

“The Shipping Wars.”

---

Thorne raises an eyebrow.

---

**Thorne**

“You're telling me romantic gossip caused a mythological breach?”

---

Casey smiles faintly.

---

**Casey**

“Emotion is energy, Commander.”

“And Saint Radian produces an extraordinary amount of it.”

---

Thorne studies the combat footage now.

The moment Elias freezes the Centaur.

Kaelen striking it.

DeMarco igniting it.

---

**Thorne**

“Your students handled themselves well.”

---

Finch nods.

---

**Finch**

“They were trained to.”

---

Casey pauses the footage on Alexander.

The moment he pulls Spencer while the Centaur charges.

---

**Casey**

“This one is the anomaly.”

---

Finch stiffens slightly.

---

**Finch**

“The sorting system flagged him.”

---

Thorne steps closer to the projection.

---

**Thorne**

“An anomaly appears.”

“Then hours later a breach occurs.”

He looks at Finch.

“You don’t see a pattern there?”

---

Finch says nothing.

---

Casey finally speaks.

---

**Casey**

“Patterns are forming across multiple variables.”

“Dreamfield instability.”

“Archetypal convergence.”

“Unregistered actors moving through the system.”

---

Thorne frowns.

---

**Thorne**

“You mean the rumors?”

---

Casey nods.

---

**Casey**

“The masked judge.”

---

Finch looks uneasy now.

---

**Finch**

“A myth invented by frightened students.”

---

Casey turns toward him.

---

**Casey**

“Perhaps.”

A pause.

Then quietly:

“Or perhaps someone else is beginning to notice the machine.”

---

The projection changes again.

Now showing the Centaur carcass discovered later.

Partially dissected.

---

Thorne's expression hardens.

---

**Thorne**

“This was not done by a student.”

---

Casey folds his hands.

---

**Casey**

“No.”

“Which means someone accessed our containment chain.”

---

Silence fills the room.

Then Thorne says one thing:

---

**Thorne**

“Find them.”

---

This conversation establishes several critical elements:

### 1. Casey and Thorne as higher authorities

They clearly outrank Finch in strategic matters.

---

### 2. The Order is not fully in control

Something interfered with the Centaur containment.

---

### 3. The Masked Judge mystery begins

The rumor reaches the highest levels.

---

### 4. Alexander becomes a variable in their system

Casey begins watching him closely.
