# Battle Eternal — Season Architecture System

## Purpose

The Season Architecture System organizes how a full season unfolds.

It ensures consistent pacing, escalating mystery, and satisfying climaxes.

Each season follows a **three-act structure across episodes**.

---

# Season Structure

A typical season contains:

`10–12 episodes`

These episodes follow a macro narrative arc.

---

# Act I — Arrival

Episodes 1–3

Purpose:

- introduce characters
    
- establish Saint Radian
    
- reveal early mysteries
    

Audience understanding:

`This is an elite academy with strange secrets.`

Mystery seeds are planted.

---

# Act II — Revelation

Episodes 4–8

Purpose:

- alliances form
    
- secrets deepen
    
- hidden systems revealed
    

Audience understanding:

`The academy is manipulating students.`

Power structures begin to emerge.

---

# Act III — Convergence

Episodes 9–12

Purpose:

- major truth revealed
    
- conflict escalates
    
- season climax occurs
    

Audience understanding:

`Saint Radian is connected to something much larger.`

---

# Episode Escalation Ladder

Each episode escalates stakes.

`Episode 1 — Orientation Episode 2 — First anomaly Episode 3 — Hidden rules Episode 4 — Rivalries emerge Episode 5 — Secret discovery Episode 6 — Institutional conflict Episode 7 — Personal crisis Episode 8 — Hidden truth Episode 9 — System revealed Episode 10 — Season climax`

---

# Character Development Flow

Each main character must progress through:

`Introduction Conflict Transformation Revelation`

---

# Season Mystery Engine

A season introduces **three major mysteries**.

Example:

`Mystery 1 — Sigmatix anomaly Mystery 2 — The Division Engine Mystery 3 — F-Link evolution`

Each episode reveals **small pieces** of these mysteries.

---

# Season Climax

The final episode must answer one major question while creating a new one.

Example:

`Answer: What the Dark Hold contains Question: Why the Order built it`
