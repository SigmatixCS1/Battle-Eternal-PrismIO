# Bound-to-Turn Mystery Engine

## Purpose

The **Bound-to-Turn Mystery Engine** controls how hidden truths gradually reveal themselves across the narrative.

Each mystery begins as a **localized anomaly** and eventually exposes a **systemic truth**.

Every mystery must progress through five stages:

`Whisper Anomaly Pattern Exposure Irreversible Truth`

These stages operate across **episodes, arcs, and seasons**.

---

# Core Mystery Arcs

Battle Eternal contains three primary mystery engines.

`1. The Sin-Eater Arc 2. The F-Link Manipulation Arc 3. The Order of the Black Sun Arc`

These arcs intersect but reveal themselves at different speeds.

---

# Mystery Engine 1

# The Sin-Eater Arc

## Narrative Function

The **Sin-Eater Arc** exposes the hidden moral corruption of the Hegemony.

It reveals that the elite are transferring their karmic burden onto disposable scholarship students called **Hollows** through the **Scapegoat Protocol**.

This arc runs in the background of Season 1 and continues through Season 2.

---

# The Scapegoat Protocol

The system is based on **Karmic Displacement**.

Elite individuals offload **Neuro-Entropic Weight** through the F-Link network into hosts called Hollows.

These hosts:

- absorb trauma
    
- experience nightmares
    
- suffer psychological collapse
    
- eventually die from **Neuro-Entropic overload**
    

The elite appear spiritually clean because the data has not yet been permanently committed to their Soul Block.

---

# Mystery Progression

## Stage 1 — Whisper

Early signs of something wrong.

Students notice:

- scholarship students collapsing
    
- rumors of "Academic Burnout"
    
- strange fatigue and nightmares among Dust Zone recruits
    

The campus begins whispering about a figure called:

`The Auditor`

No one knows if this figure is real.

---

## Stage 2 — Anomaly

The pattern deepens.

Events occur that cannot be explained:

- scholarship students die suddenly
    
- wealthy elites suffer neurological breakdowns
    
- both events occur at the same time
    

The connection is invisible but real.

---

## Stage 3 — Pattern

Certain characters begin noticing the pattern.

Key investigators:

`Grant Sterling Spencer Kordell Sigmatix`

Grant’s **Parallax Node system** detects infrastructure anomalies within the F-Link network.

Spencer begins tracking institutional inconsistencies.

Sigmatix observes the pattern before it becomes visible.

---

## Stage 4 — Exposure

The system becomes visible.

The truth emerges:

`Hollows are carrying elite karmic burden.`

The figure known as **The Auditor** is killing these hosts in order to:

- sever the karmic transfer
    
- return the guilt to its origin
    

This creates the central moral question of the arc:

`Is the Auditor a murderer or a liberator?`

The narrative intentionally refuses to answer this definitively.

---

## Stage 5 — Irreversible Truth

The arc culminates in the **Blackout Protocol**.

Sable Dynamics attempts a mass offloading event that would permanently transfer elite guilt into hundreds of Hollows.

Grant intervenes and reverses the system architecture, creating a **Karmic Backdraft** that exposes elites to the suffering they attempted to offload.

The system collapses.

But the truth cannot be hidden anymore.

---

Their actions restore balance when systemic injustice becomes intolerable.
