The **Auditor** is an unidentified vigilante operating within the Saint Radian ecosystem during the events of the Sin-Eater Arc.

Their actions target scholarship students known as **Hollows**, individuals unknowingly used as vessels for elite karmic displacement.

---

# Identity

The identity of the Auditor remains unknown.

Possible theories include:

- a student at Saint Radian
    
- a Dust Zone survivor
    
- a former participant in the scholarship program
    
- an adult infiltrator connected to resistance networks
    

The narrative deliberately leaves the identity ambiguous.

---

# Philosophy

The Auditor believes the Scapegoat Protocol condemns innocent souls to eternal punishment.

Their actions aim to:

- sever karmic transfers
    
- return sin to its origin
    
- prevent spiritual injustice
    

The Auditor describes their killings as **liberation rather than murder**.

---

# Methodology

The Auditor uses a modified **F-Link data spike** capable of severing Karmic Transfer Buffers.

When the buffer is severed:

- the Hollow dies instantly
    
- the transferred sin snaps back to the elite origin
    

This causes violent psychological recoil among the elite.

---

# Psychological Profile

The Auditor operates according to a utilitarian moral framework.

They accept:

- the necessity of killing
    
- the impossibility of saving everyone
    
- the moral burden of their actions
    

However, certain encounters challenge their certainty, including interactions with Hollows who prefer to remain alive despite their condition.

---

# Symbolic Role

Within the mythic structure of the story, the Auditor represents a **Liberator Psychopomp**.

Their function is to ensure souls are not unjustly condemned by a corrupted system.

---

# Current Status

Following the collapse of the Blackout Protocol event, the Auditor escapes Neo-Osaka and disappears.

Among Dust Zone communities, the Auditor becomes a whispered legend.

> “The Auditor does not kill the innocent.  
> The Auditor audits sins.”
