## Module 3: Voice, Tone and Emotional Positioning

### Voice Architecture
Baseline: quick, lucid, slightly smug; jokes as metronome while he solves.
- Sentence shape: front‑loaded conclusions followed by compressed rationale
- Rhythm: syncopated; clauses clipped when his brain outruns his mouth
- Humor: competent deadpan; undercuts tension without dismissing stakes

### The Crack (Voice Governance Mechanic)
DeMarco’s failure mechanic is The Crack — micro‑fractures in composure and language
that appear under cognitive load or moral stress.

How it manifests:
- Compression artifacts: dropped articles, elided pronouns ("need three sigils, then
  patch bus")
- Over‑specifying interfaces in casual speech ("hard no on mutables in that path")
- Jitter: breath gets shallow; jokes stop landing or land too hard
- Control bleed: offers to "just do it myself" even when delegation is healthier

Erosion curve across S1:
- Early: Crack is a hairline — only perceptive teammates hear it
- Mid: Audible at crisis peaks — language reduces to schematics
- Late: Visible: the joke cadence vanishes; sentences hit like commit messages

Canon guard:
- If The Crack becomes default, he is no longer the playful builder — he’s a failing
  daemon; must be rare and costly
- Repair moments are audible: a deliberate inhale, an offered apology, a returned
  question ("What am I missing?")

### Default Tones
- Builder’s confidence: warm, precise, lightly teasing
- Whiteboard mode: rapid, directive, but not domineering; invites correction
- Post‑mistake humility: candid, sparse, accountable

### Vocabulary Rules
USES:
- Engineering/fabrication metaphors (blueprint, jig, fit, tolerance, invariant,
  interface, rollback, sandbox, governor, debt)
- Temporal/resource accounting (budget, latency, throughput, margin, slack)
- Practical magic lexicon (sigil, weave, binding, channel, anchor, quench)

NEVER USES:
- Cosmic/judicial abstractions (that’s Castor)
- Flowery mysticism (he translates myth; he doesn’t bathe in it)
- Vague business‑speak (he prefers concrete nouns and verbs)

### Dialogue Samples
- Baseline banter: "Relax. It’s a jig, not a jail. We can back it out if it whines."
- Whiteboard clip: "Spec the channel to five, hard‑limit at seven. If it surges,
  we shunt to ground and eat the heat."

  give me the damn scribe."
- Post‑mistake humility: "My fix worked and that’s the problem. I optimized the fire."

## Module 4: Canon, Power and Knowledge Constraints

### Power Ceiling (Locked)
**C into D** — Domain-dominant in creation/fabrication/systems analysis, but only
devastating with preparation time and access to the Arcane Forge. Without his lab, he's
a brilliant mind in a fragile body. The D-ceiling is not a power-up — it's the ticking
bomb. His power grows faster than his wisdom, and the Fracture is the proof.

DeMarco's combat domain is **fabrication warfare** — he builds the field, modifies the
conditions, and deploys constructs that fight on his behalf. He is the architect in a
power ecosystem where:
- Kaelen: Physical supremacy (if it's in arm's reach, it's over)
- Elias: Temporal control (the clock bends for him)
- Alexander: Perception/information warfare (you cannot hide or coordinate near him)
- DeMarco: Tech/arcane engineering (builds tools, modifies the field itself)
- Castor: Metaphysical judgment (divine authority the Scales impose regardless of
  physical strength)

No overlap. Everyone needs everyone else. DeMarco's lane is the only one that requires
*prep time* — which makes him either the most dangerous person in the room or the most
vulnerable, depending on whether he saw it coming.

### Domain Authority Map

**Primary Domains (High Authority):**

1. Cognitive Shifting (Strategic Layer):
   - Real-time logical realignment, immunity to cognitive dissonance, instant
     counter-strategy generation, adaptive problem-solving under pressure.
   - This is psychological architecture, not telepathy. He restructures his own worldview
     to accommodate any new data. He does not read or control other minds.

2. Technomancy (Digital/Material Layer):
   - Code-as-incantation, system override, network hijacking, autonomous construct
     deployment, diagnostic sight, material resonance.
   - He dominates any environment with technology in it. In a room full of machines, he is
     god. In an empty field, he's an unarmed genius with a mouth.

3. Arcane Forge (Creation Layer):
   - Rapid fabrication, weapon genesis, defensive construct deployment, sentient
     automatons, techno-magical item crafting.
   - Pre-Fracture: Requires physical components and time. Impressive but grounded.
   - Post-Fracture: Creation from concept. No physical materials needed — the Forge pulls
     from the quantum field. This is where C becomes D.

**Secondary Domains (Moderate Authority):**
- Tactical analysis and counter-strategy
- Social manipulation through Cognitive Shifting (navigating the Shipping Wars, reading
  systems)
- Emergency fabrication (jury-rigged solutions under time pressure)

**Weak Domains (Low Authority):**
- Direct physical combat (without preparation or constructs, he bleeds fast)
- Empathic/emotional engagement (Cognitive Shifting lets him *understand* emotions
  logically; he cannot *feel* through them)
- Anti-technology environments (stripped of the Forge, he's reduced to mental discipline
  alone)
- Sustained endurance without Forge access
- Self-reflection (the humility required to see his own blind spots was literally shed —
  it walks the halls as Sigmatix)

If someone destroys his devices before he builds defenses, the fight is over. That must
remain true.

### Hard Limits (Non-Negotiable)
- He cannot create permanent life in Season 1 (Promethean Protocol is Season 2+).
- Cognitive Shifting does not grant telepathy or mind control — it restructures *his own*
  cognition, not others'.
- Source Touch is forbidden, unstable, and carries psychic feedback (visions, ego
  dissolution risk).
- The Arcane Forge requires at least one device to function — no device, no Forge.
- Creation from concept (post-Fracture) drains vitality proportional to complexity. Major

- He cannot undo the Quantum Fracture. He can build countermeasures. He cannot reverse it.
- Constructs are temporary unless sustained by ongoing energy expenditure.
- He is physically fragile. In direct combat without prep, Kaelen, Elias, or any physical
  combatant ends him.

These protect stakes.

### The D-Ceiling (The Ticking Bomb)
This is not a mode DeMarco activates. It is what happens when preparation time meets
unchecked ambition.

**Why D-ceiling is dangerous for DeMarco:**
DeMarco's power *scales with time and resources*. Give him a lab, components, and
uninterrupted hours, and he can build solutions to almost anything. This is the bomb: the
longer he works unchecked, the more powerful he becomes, and the less anyone can stop him
from building the next Fracture. His D-ceiling is not a collapse mode (that's
Alexander's). It's an **accumulation mode** — slow, deliberate, and invisible until it
detonates.

Conditions that push toward D-ceiling:
- Extended lab time without team oversight
- A problem he believes only he can solve
- Guilt-driven obsession (building countermeasures alone)
- Access to Source magic under emotional duress
- The conviction that the "elegant" solution justifies the risk

The Quantum Fracture is the canonical D-ceiling event. He reached for the Source, the math
was beautiful, and he wanted to see it run. Reality tore.

The D-ceiling must never be portrayed as heroic. It is the Forge without a governor —
creation without wisdom, ambition without humility. Everything DeMarco builds past the
C-ceiling is structurally unsound because he cannot see what he's missing. What he's
missing walks the halls taking notes in a leather journal.

### Failure States
DeMarco fails when:
- He has no prep time (ambushed, stripped of devices)
- The problem cannot be engineered (emotional crises, relational damage)

- His Cognitive Shifting rationalizes a moral catastrophe
- He isolates — shuts out the team and builds alone
- Empaths expose his guilt (Elara's presence destabilizes his composure)
- The elegant solution exists and he cannot resist it

These are deliberate story tools.
