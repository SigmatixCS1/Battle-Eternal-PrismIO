# The Scapegoat Protocol — Mythic Context

The **Scapegoat Protocol** represents the technological evolution of ancient rituals designed to displace moral responsibility.

Throughout human history, societies attempted to remove sin through symbolic transfers.

Examples include:

- the Sin-Eater ritual
    
- the biblical scapegoat
    
- confession and absolution rites
    
- sacrificial purification ceremonies
    

The Scapegoat Protocol industrializes this concept through the **F-Link system**.

---

# Mechanism

Elite individuals transfer their accumulated **Neuro-Entropic Weight** into selected hosts known as **Hollows**.

These hosts:

- absorb trauma
    
- experience nightmares
    
- undergo progressive psychological collapse
    

The elite appear spiritually clean because the data has not yet been permanently committed to their Soul Block.

---

# The Mythic Violation

The Protocol represents a fundamental violation of cosmic law.

Sin is not meant to be transferable.

By engineering a system that redirects karmic burden, the corporations attempt to **cheat metaphysical judgment itself**.

---

# Emergence of the Auditor

The Auditor appears as a disruptive force within the system.

Rather than accepting the ritual, the Auditor:

- severs karmic buffers
    
- returns guilt to its origin
    
- prevents innocent souls from carrying foreign sins
    

In mythological terms, the Auditor represents **the collapse of a corrupted absolution ritual**.

---

# Narrative Significance

The Scapegoat Protocol demonstrates that the greatest horror of the Hegemony is not technological power.

It is the belief that **guilt can be erased rather than confronted**.
