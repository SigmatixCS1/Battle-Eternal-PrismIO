# Design Principle

The [[Order of the Black Sun]] has never operated openly. For 400 years, following [[Isabella de la Fonte]]'s model, they have wrapped themselves in successive layers of corporate, institutional, and legal camouflage. Each layer appears legitimate to the one below it. No single layer can see more than one level above.

The result: the public interacts with a technology company; the executives of that company answer to a conglomerate; the conglomerate answers to a holding group; the holding group answers to a trust; the trust is controlled by a shadow network that predates all of them by centuries.

---

## The Hierarchy (Bottom to Top)

### Level 1: Consumer-Facing — Functionality Link Systems (FLS)

**Public Name:** Functionality Link Systems  
**Common Name:** "F-Link" (the product IS the brand)  
**Type:** Neural technology corporation  
**Public HQ:** Neo-Osaka Innovation District  
**CEO (Public-Facing):** A rotating figurehead approved by Level 2. Charismatic, media-trained, disposable.

This is what citizens interact with. FLS manufactures [[F-Link]] neural implants, runs the consumer platform, operates the F-Link Symposiums, and provides "customer support." Their branding is sleek, utopian, and relentlessly positive — *"Connectivity is Unity."* FLS employees genuinely believe they work for a tech company improving lives. Only the C-suite knows they answer to someone else.

**Sin-Eater Arc Relevance:** This is the entity the original synopsis called "F-Corp." Director Kaelus holds the position of FLS Regional Director — high enough to access karmic displacement services, low enough to be expendable.

---

### Level 2: Technology Conglomerate — Sable Dynamics Corporation

**Type:** Multinational neural technology and consciousness engineering conglomerate  
**Public HQ:** Zurich (European legitimacy)  
**Chairman:** Board-appointed, [[The Concordance|Concordance]] member (hereditary seat)

Sable Dynamics is the parent company of FLS. It also controls:

- **Sable Neurotech** — R&D division (consciousness interface research, neural chip fabrication)
- **Sable BioMetrics** — Biometric security and identity systems
- **Cognitive Wellness Partners** — "Mental health" subsidiary that runs the scholarship programs and "neural optimization therapy" (the cover for the Hollow program in the Sin-Eater arc)
- **Sable Defence Systems** — Military/security applications of F-Link technology

Sable Dynamics funds and co-operates the [[Synthesis Laboratories]] at [[The Academy of Saint Radian|Saint Radian]]. Their executives eat at [[The Gilded Fork]]. They know F-Link does more than "connect" — they understand it as consciousness optimization infrastructure. They do **NOT** know about the [[The Dark Hold|Dark Hold]], soul blocks, or the metaphysical harvesting. They think they're building the future of human cognition.

**Etymology:** "Sable" = black in heraldry. An inside joke among Concordance members. To everyone else, it's just a brand name.

---

### Level 3: Multi-Sector Holding Company — The Meridian Group

**Type:** Global infrastructure conglomerate  
**Registered:** Multiple jurisdictions (legal labyrinth by design)  
**Board:** Concordance-appointed. Interlocking directorships with [[The Hegemony|Hegemony]] governance bodies.

The Meridian Group is the economic engine of the Hegemony. It owns Sable Dynamics alongside the other pillars of the Hegemony's infrastructure:

- **Sable Dynamics Corporation** → Neural technology ([[F-Link]])
- **Prisma Media Networks** → Media/entertainment (public distribution arm of the [[Division Engine]])
- **NutriCore Distribution** → Food systems (Nutri-Paste manufacturing, real food supply chains)
- **Aethon Transit Systems** → Transportation ([[The Mag-Lev Serpent|Mag-Lev Serpent]], transit infrastructure)
- **The Saint Radian Educational Trust** → Education ([[The Academy of Saint Radian|Functionality Academies]], Saint Radian funding)
- **Various smaller subsidiaries** → Including the [[The Neon Lotus Arcade|Neon Lotus Arcade]]'s parent company

The Meridian Group IS the Hegemony's economy. When the Hegemony passes a law requiring F-Link for employment, it's a Meridian subsidiary that profits. When Nutri-Paste is the only food available, NutriCore controls the supply. The government and the corporation are indistinguishable — by design.

Meridian executives know the system is authoritarian. They frame it as "necessary governance for a complex world." Some are true believers in order; others are simply wealthy enough not to care. They attend Concordance galas but believe The Concordance is merely an elite philanthropic society.

---

### Level 4: Shadow Governance — The Concordance

**Type:** Intergenerational trust, advisory council, and shadow parliament  
**Founded:** 17th century by [[Isabella de la Fonte]]  
**Public Face:** The Concordance Foundation — a philanthropic governance advisory body that "consults" with world leaders  
**True Function:** Controls The Meridian Group through a web of shell corporations, blind trusts, proxy voting structures, and hereditary board seats

The Concordance is where the corporate hierarchy ends and the occult hierarchy begins. Its membership is hereditary — the great families descended from (or absorbed by) the Order over 400 years. Concordance members include:

- Heads of Meridian Group subsidiaries
- Hegemony government ministers
- [[Church of Predestination]] senior clergy
- Academy headmasters ([[Alistair Finch]] holds a Concordance seat)
- Senior military and intelligence figures

Concordance members know the system is designed as a control mechanism. They know F-Link is more than technology. They know emotional harvesting is real. Most believe this is simply the price of maintaining civilization — *"Someone must shepherd the masses."* They do **NOT** all know about the Dark Hold's metaphysical nature, the [[The Crimson Convergence|Crimson Convergence]], or the existence of the [[The Furies|Furies]] as literal entities. Those secrets belong to the final layer.

**The Concordance Council:** An inner circle of 13 seats (mirroring traditional occult governance). Seven are hereditary; six rotate among the most powerful Concordance families. The Council approves all major Meridian Group decisions, Academy appointments, and Hegemony policy directives.

---

### Level 5: Occult Command — The Order of the Black Sun

**Type:** Ancient occult shadow network  
**Founded:** 17th century (formalized), but its esoteric lineage stretches much further back  
**Leader:** [[Cassian Thorne]] (The Architect)  
**Structure:** Archbishops → Regional Priests → Field Agents → Sleeper Assets

The [[Order of the Black Sun]] controls The Concordance through its Archbishops, who hold the seven hereditary Council seats. The Order is the purpose behind every layer below it. They know everything:

- F-Link binds souls to the [[The Dark Hold|Dark Hold]] blockchain
- Emotional harvesting fuels the [[The Crimson Convergence|Crimson Convergence]]
- The [[The Furies|Furies]] are literal metaphysical entities managing the [[Division Engine]]
- The entire corporate-governmental infrastructure exists to feed Server Zero

The Order predates the corporate structure by intent — Isabella de la Fonte didn't create the Order's need for corporate camouflage; she created the solution. The corporations, the Hegemony, the Concordance — these are all just the latest iteration of a system that has taken different forms across centuries. In the 17th century it was trade guilds and banking houses. In the 19th century it was industrial empires. Now it is a seamless corporate-governmental fusion that most people simply call "the way things are."

## How the Layers Interact

### Information Flows UP, Control Flows DOWN

- **Level 1** (FLS) reports user data and integration metrics to **Level 2** (Sable)
- **Level 2** reports consciousness research breakthroughs to **Level 3** (Meridian)
- **Level 3** reports economic and social control metrics to **Level 4** (Concordance)
- **Level 4** reports to **Level 5** (the Order), which issues directives back down through the chain

### Compartmentalization is Absolute

- An FLS engineer knows nothing about Sable's defence contracts
- A Sable VP doesn't know about Meridian's food distribution monopoly connection
- A Meridian board member doesn't know the Concordance is anything more than a philanthropy
- Most Concordance members don't know the Order exists as an occult entity
- Only the Order's inner circle knows the full picture
