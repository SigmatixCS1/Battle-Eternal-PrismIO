# Global Power Factions

The world of Battle Eternal is shaped by seven dominant power structures that compete for influence over humanity’s future.

Some operate openly through political or religious institutions, while others exist entirely in shadow.

---

# The Seven Factions

1. Neo-Osaka Technate
2. Western Hegemony Council
3. Dominion Directorate
4. Apostolic Communion
5. Purity Orders
6. Southern Coalition Assembly
7. Order of the Black Sun

---

# Neo-Osaka Technate

Technological superpower centered in the Pacific Nexus.

Belief: humanity must evolve through technology.

Key technology: F-Link neural integration network.

---

# Western Hegemony Council

Corporate alliance controlling global finance and media.

Belief: markets should govern society.

---

# Dominion Directorate

Authoritarian superstate prioritizing stability and control.

Belief: individual freedom leads to instability.

---

# Apostolic Communion

Technotheological religious movement.

Sacrament: neural chip communion.

Ritual phrase:

"Unify with God."

---

# Purity Orders

Anti-technology religious resistance movement.

Belief: neural implants corrupt human nature.

---

# Southern Coalition Assembly

Alliance of developing nations seeking independence from global superpowers.

---

# Order of the Black Sun

Secret organization manipulating global events.

Most world leaders are unaware of its existence.
