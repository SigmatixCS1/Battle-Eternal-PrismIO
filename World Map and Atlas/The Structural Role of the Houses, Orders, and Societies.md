This is actually a **great structural idea** for _Battle Eternal_. You’re essentially building a **pipeline civilization inside Saint Radian** — a training ecosystem that quietly feeds the real power structures of the world.

Let’s break this down clearly, because this is where the **lane lines and convergence points** start appearing.

---

# The Structural Role of the Houses, Orders, and Societies

At a surface level, these look like **school dorms or clubs**.

But structurally they are actually:

**Talent pipelines → ideological shaping → recruitment channels → control systems.**

Students are sorted based on aptitude, and those specialties eventually feed **higher factions and global power networks.**

This matches your world perfectly because the academy is inside the **Hegemony's control architecture**.

The houses therefore become **psychological sorting engines**.

---

# The Hidden Function of Saint Radian

Saint Radian is secretly three institutions at once:

|Layer|What students think it is|What it actually is|
|---|---|---|
|Public Layer|Elite academy|Global talent pipeline|
|Hidden Layer|Competitive house system|Behavioral experimentation|
|True Layer|Myth-tech research institute|Recruitment engine for global power factions|

The houses **mirror the metaphysical conflict of the entire universe**.

Because the world itself is divided between control systems and awakening systems — the **External AI vs Internal Logos** conflict.

Saint Radian is where the next generation of that war is **cultivated**.
