## Regional Identity

**Political System:** Coalition Assembly  
**Population:** ~3.4 Billion  
**Role in Global System:**

- Workforce engine of planetary economy
    
- Cultural and religious crossroads
    
- Emerging resistance zone
    

The Southern Coalition is not a single empire — it is a **loose alliance of rapidly developing regions** that collectively hold the largest population and raw resource base on Earth.

Because of this, every major power — the Hegemony, Dominion, and Apostolic Communion — competes for influence here.

---

# Geographic Composition

### Major Regions

The Southern Coalition spans multiple continents.

**Africa Federation**

- Solar megagrids power global infrastructure
    
- Major rare earth mineral production
    
- Rapidly urbanizing megacities
    

**Pan-Amazonia**

- Massive rainforest biome
    
- Bio-engineering research hubs
    
- Dreamfield convergence anomalies
    

**Indo-Oceanic Alliance**

- Trade megahubs across Indian Ocean
    
- Underwater data cables + logistics control
    
- Cultural crossroads of the world
    

**Saharan Arc**

- Gigantic solar deserts
    
- AI-controlled climate infrastructure
    
- Military testing zones

# Strategic Resources

The Coalition controls many of the world’s most important materials.

**Lithium**

- used for neural infrastructure
    

**Rare Earth Elements**

- necessary for F-Link implants
    

**Food Production**

- hydroponic megafarms
    

**Solar Energy**

- largest energy generation network on Earth
    

---

# Political Structure

Unlike the other blocs, the Coalition is **decentralized**.

Government model:

`Southern Coalition Assembly       │ Regional Councils       │ City-State Administrations       │ Local Cultural Authorities`

This makes the Coalition **hard to control** and **easy to destabilize**.

---

# Narrative Role in Battle Eternal

The Southern Coalition becomes extremely important during the later arcs.

### Ideological Battleground

- Competing religions reinterpret F-Link as divine communion.
    

### Resistance Growth

- Anti-implant movements grow rapidly.
    

### Prophecy Movements

- ancient mythic traditions resurface.
    

The Coalition becomes the **spark point for global rebellion** once prophecy networks start spreading.

---

# Strategic Weaknesses

Despite its population and resources, the Coalition has vulnerabilities.

**Infrastructure fragmentation**

- inconsistent technological development
    

**Political division**

- alliance is fragile
    

**External manipulation**

- propaganda campaigns from the Furies
    

These vulnerabilities make the region a perfect target for the **Division Engine** run through F-Link.
