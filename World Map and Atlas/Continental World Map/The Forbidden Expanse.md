# The Forbidden Expanse

### Mysterious Anomaly Zones of the Battle Eternal World

Across the planet lie territories officially designated **Forbidden Zones** by the Hegemony.

Public explanations vary:

- environmental collapse
    
- radiation contamination
    
- military quarantine
    
- unstable tectonic zones
    

But the truth is far more disturbing.

These areas are locations where the **boundary between the Surface World and deeper layers of reality has fractured**.

Dreamfield anomalies, mythic archetypes, and Fury-level metaphysical forces leak into the physical world.

Most governments agree on one thing:

**No one truly controls these territories.**

---

# Why the Zones Exist

According to the hidden cosmology of the world, reality operates as a layered system:

Surface World  
↓  
Dreamfield  
↓  
Mythic Strata  
↓  
Fury Domains  
↓  
Dark Hold

When the barrier between these layers breaks, **an anomaly zone forms**.

Forbidden Expanse territories are therefore:

• Dreamfield rupture zones  
• ancient mythological sites  
• failed experiments by the Order of the Black Sun  
• locations where archetypal entities have manifested

---

# Major Forbidden Expanse Zones

## 1 — The Sundered Sahara

**Location:** North Africa

### Description

A vast desert region where reality behaves inconsistently.

Satellite imagery often shows **impossible geometry**:

- dunes forming fractal patterns
    
- shadows moving independently of the sun
    
- entire sandstorms appearing and disappearing in minutes
    

### Origin

During early F-Link prototype testing in the 2020s, an experimental **mass neural synchronization test** accidentally triggered a Dreamfield rupture.

Millions of human subconscious signals briefly connected.

The result:

A **permanent tear between the Dreamfield and physical world**.

### Phenomena

• Dream-creatures materializing briefly  
• auditory hallucinations shared by multiple explorers  
• buried ruins appearing in different locations each year

### World Perception

The **Sacred Belt religions** believe the region is a punishment for humanity’s technological arrogance.

The **Hegemony** labels it a radiation zone and strictly forbids entry.

---

## 2 — The Black Pacific Scar

**Location:** Mid-Pacific Ocean

### Description

An enormous circular oceanic region where:

- compasses spin
    
- satellites lose signal
    
- ships vanish
    

The sea surface sometimes appears **perfectly still for miles**, like glass.

### Origin

Ancient myth suggests this location was once a **gateway to the Fury Domains**.

Modern evidence indicates the Order of the Black Sun attempted a **Crimson Convergence ritual** there decades ago.

The ritual failed.

The ocean floor collapsed into a massive metaphysical vortex.

### Phenomena

• glowing red lightning beneath the water  
• ghost signals from lost ships  
• rare sightings of enormous shadow-like sea creatures

### World Perception

Sailors call it:

**The Dead Compass Sea**

The Hegemony maintains a permanent naval exclusion perimeter.

---

## 3 — The Siberian Silence

**Location:** Eastern Eurasia

### Description

A frozen forest spanning thousands of kilometers where **sound behaves strangely**.

People entering the zone report:

- voices echoing with no source
    
- memories replaying in auditory form
    
- conversations heard from miles away
    

### Origin

Ancient Siberian tribes considered the region a **dream-spirit crossing point**.

Later investigations discovered something else.

The region sits directly above a **massive Dreamfield convergence node**.

### Phenomena

• dream memories leaking into reality  
• travelers encountering people who later prove to be dead  
• sudden time distortions

### World Perception

The **Eurasian Dominion** treats the area as a strategic research zone.

Rumor suggests they are trying to weaponize dream manipulation.

---

## 4 — The Amazon Echo Basin

**Location:** Deep Amazon rainforest

### Description

A dense jungle where **ancient ruins appear that are not present on any maps**.

Explorers report:

- temples covered in unknown symbols
    
- statues that resemble mythological creatures from multiple cultures simultaneously
    
- entire cities vanishing overnight
    

### Origin

Archaeologists believe this region may contain **remnants of a pre-Atlantean civilization**.

The ruins seem to phase in and out of existence.

### Phenomena

• shared dream visions among expedition members  
• sudden appearance of impossible architecture  
• living vegetation responding to emotional states

### World Perception

The **Southern Coalition** officially protects the forest as a sacred ecological preserve.

Privately, they consider it the **most dangerous anomaly on Earth**.

---

## 5 — Antarctica: The Hollow Ice

**Location:** East Antarctica

### Description

Satellite scans reveal massive underground caverns beneath the ice.

Energy readings fluctuate wildly.

The region is the **least explored anomaly zone on the planet**.

### Origin

Some Concordance archives suggest this site may contain a **sealed gateway to the Dark Hold** itself.

The Order of the Black Sun maintains secret installations nearby.

### Phenomena

• gravitational disturbances  
• auroras appearing underground  
• unknown signals detected beneath the ice sheet

### World Perception

The Hegemony maintains a **global treaty banning exploration**.

In reality, multiple secret expeditions are underway.

---

# How the Continents View the Forbidden Expanse

### Western Hegemony

View the zones as **containment problems**.

They fund secret research programs but publicly claim the regions are natural disasters.

---

### Eurasian Dominion

Sees them as **strategic weapons opportunities**.

Dreamfield research labs are rumored to exist near anomaly borders.

---

### Pacific Nexus

Scientists there view the zones as **the key to understanding consciousness itself**.

Several Saint Radian researchers secretly study Dreamfield resonance patterns.

---

### Sacred Belt

Religious authorities consider the Forbidden Expanse **proof of divine judgment**.

Pilgrimage cults attempt to enter the zones despite government bans.

---

### Southern Coalition

Treats the zones with the most caution.

Many indigenous traditions already recognized these locations as **spirit boundaries** long before modern governments appeared.

# Narrative Purpose in Battle Eternal

The Forbidden Expanse is not just worldbuilding.

It serves multiple story functions:

1️⃣ **Future story arcs**  
Exploration missions, ancient ruins, Fury gateways.

2️⃣ **Mythology confirmation**  
Proof that archetypes and myths are real.

3️⃣ **Resistance discoveries**  
Characters may learn the Order cannot fully control reality.

4️⃣ **Endgame escalation**  
If the Dark Hold destabilizes, these zones will expand.
