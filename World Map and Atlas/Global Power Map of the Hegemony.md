Great question. Expanding the **global geopolitical layer** will make _Battle Eternal_ feel much larger than just Neo-Osaka. The key is to remember one canon rule you already established:

- The **Hegemony is the official global government**, but it operates through **regional corporate blocs and administrative regimes**.
    
- Those blocs control **resources**, **technology infrastructure**, and **F-Link data centers**, which gives them leverage inside the system.
    

So each continent can function like a **semi-autonomous Hegemony Directorate** with a different political model, economic resource base, and tactical advantage.

Below is a **world map structure that fits your lore**.

---

# Global Power Map of the Hegemony

Officially there is **one government**.

In reality, power is distributed across **Corporate Directorates** aligned with the **Order of the Black Sun**.

Each continent functions as a **regional control engine**.

---

# North America

## The Algorithmic Directorate

### Political System

Technocratic Corporate Governance

North America is run by a council of **mega-corporations integrated directly with F-Link infrastructure**.

Voting still exists but it is mostly ceremonial.  
Policy decisions are made by **predictive governance algorithms**.

### Major Corporations

- Meridian Group
    
- Sable Dynamics
    
- Atlas NeuroSystems
    

### Resources

- AI compute infrastructure
    
- Quantum data centers
    
- Advanced robotics
    
- Defense technology
    

### Tactical Advantage

**Global Data Dominance**

North America controls the **largest F-Link data clusters**, allowing it to:

- monitor emotional output worldwide
    
- run predictive models on social behavior
    
- preempt resistance movements
    

This is essentially the **brain of the Hegemony**.

---

# Europe

## The Cultural Compliance Directorate

### Political System

Technocratic Parliamentary Federation

Europe presents itself as the **ethical face of the Hegemony**.

It maintains elaborate regulatory frameworks and cultural institutions to legitimize the system.

### Resources

- cultural media networks
    
- financial markets
    
- diplomatic institutions
    
- propaganda production
    

### Tactical Advantage

**Narrative Control**

Europe controls:

- global entertainment
    
- historical archives
    
- mythological reinterpretation
    

This is where the **Narrative Compliance Board** is strongest.

Their job is to shape what humanity **believes about reality**.

---

# East Asia

## The Cybernetic Harmony Directorate

### Political System

State-Corporate Fusion

East Asia is the most technologically integrated region.

Governments and corporations function as a single structure.

### Resources

- semiconductor production
    
- neural interface manufacturing
    
- surveillance infrastructure
    
- cybernetic implants
    

### Tactical Advantage

**F-Link Hardware Production**

Nearly every F-Link implant is manufactured here.

This gives East Asia control over:

- hardware upgrades
    
- firmware updates
    
- neural interface compatibility
    

Neo-Osaka exists here for a reason.

It’s the **technological epicenter of the Hegemony**.

---

# Africa

## The Resource Sovereignty Bloc

### Political System

Corporate Extraction Protectorates

Africa is officially part of the Hegemony but operates with **regional autonomy due to its resource wealth**.

Several regions are controlled by corporate protectorates rather than governments.

### Resources

- rare earth minerals
    
- cobalt
    
- lithium
    
- energy reserves
    
- solar infrastructure
    

### Tactical Advantage

**Energy and Materials Monopoly**

Africa controls the **raw materials required for advanced technology**.

Without African resources:

- AI hardware cannot be produced
    
- batteries cannot be built
    
- F-Link hardware supply collapses
    

This gives them enormous leverage.

---

# South America

## The Biotech Sovereignty Alliance

### Political System

Eco-Technocratic Confederation

South America developed a hybrid system combining **environmental governance with biotech research**.

The Hegemony allows some autonomy because the region is critical for biological resources.

### Resources

- pharmaceuticals
    
- genetic engineering
    
- agricultural biotech
    
- biodiversity research
    

### Tactical Advantage

**Biological Innovation**

South America leads the world in:

- genetic therapies
    
- bioengineered crops
    
- neural bio-interfaces
    

Many **experimental F-Link neural adaptation protocols** were developed here.

---

# Middle East

## The Energy Custodian States

### Political System

Corporate-Royal Alliances

Traditional monarchies merged with corporate energy conglomerates.

The region functions as the **energy control hub of the Hegemony**.

### Resources

- oil
    
- hydrogen fuel
    
- fusion reactor infrastructure
    
- desalination networks
    

### Tactical Advantage

**Global Energy Control**

Energy supply chains originate here.

This gives the region influence over:

- transportation networks
    
- industrial production
    
- military logistics
    

---

# Oceania

## The Strategic Security Directorate

### Political System

Military-Corporate Security State

Oceania operates as the **defense perimeter of the Hegemony**.

Many islands function as classified research and surveillance bases.

### Resources

- satellite networks
    
- naval infrastructure
    
- drone manufacturing
    
- orbital defense systems
    

### Tactical Advantage

**Global Surveillance Grid**

Oceania controls:

- satellite constellations
    
- orbital weapons platforms
    
- drone fleets
    

They provide the **eyes of the Hegemony**.

---

# Antarctica

## The Black Sun Sanctum

### Political System

Classified Territory

Officially a scientific research zone.

In reality it houses **the deepest infrastructure of the Order of the Black Sun**.

### Resources

- hidden research facilities
    
- Dark Hold infrastructure
    
- ley-line convergence sites
    

### Tactical Advantage

**Metaphysical Control**

Antarctica contains:

- ancient ruins
    
- ley-line intersections
    
- the deepest access nodes to **Server Zero**
    

This makes it the **spiritual heart of the Order’s power**.

# Global Strategic Balance

Each region dominates a different layer of power.

|Region|Power Domain|
|---|---|
|North America|Data & AI|
|Europe|Culture & Narrative|
|East Asia|Hardware & Cybernetics|
|Africa|Resources & Energy Materials|
|South America|Biotech|
|Middle East|Energy|
|Oceania|Surveillance & Military|
|Antarctica|Occult Infrastructure|

Together these systems create a **planetary control network**.
