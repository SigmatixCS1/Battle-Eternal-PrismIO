## Overview
The **Geometricum Order** pursues the universal **archetypal blueprints** underlying all of Saint Radian’s mysteries. To them, the **Flower of Life**, **Metatron’s Cube**, and **Vesica Piscis** are not symbols but living equations that can be used to stabilize or destabilize reality.  

Where [[House_Living_Sigils|Living Sigils]] inscribe glyphs in vines, roots, and natural mediums, the Geometricum Order deals in **pure form** — crystalline lattices, projection fields, and resonance grids. They are often accused of being "cold mathematicians of the soul," but their work provides some of the most powerful containment, projection, and resonance technologies in the narrative war.

---

## Dormitory
**The Fractal Hall** — a crystalline dome whose walls rearrange into recursive Flower-of-Life tessellations. Its architecture hums in resonance, shifting during meditation hours to amplify student subconscious states.

---

## Practices
- **Channeling through Form:** Students draw and activate sacred geometries in trance, allowing archetypes to stabilize (or collapse) within resonance fields.
- **Fractal Pathworking:** Mandalas of geometry are used as gateways; advanced initiates can walk these "paths" into archetypal domains.
- **Consciousness Projection:** The **Merkaba Spindle** is the Order’s crown practice: projection vehicles of light built from interlocking tetrahedrons allow travel between dreamscapes and Logos-wrapped realms.

---

## Signature Relics & Geometry
- **Seed of Life Channeler** → Used during initiation meditation, focusing Logos spark into stable archetypal grounding.
- **Metatron’s Cube Wards** → Incredibly dangerous geometric fields that compress multiple archetypes into cubes of resonance. Utilized by Order strike-mages and often banned.
- **Merkaba Spindle** → Interlocking tetrahedrons forming an astral vehicle for limited projection between realms.
- **64 Tetrahedron Grid** → Esoteric form said to simulate infinite stability; experimental, highly classified.

---

## Rivalries & Philosophy Conflicts
- **House of Living Sigils:**  
  Allies yet rivals. Living Sigils believe geometry is meaningless without life as a host; Geometricum insists biology simply imperfectly imitates the eternal forms.
- **Aetherion Society:**  
  Compete for the “mathematics of Logos.” The Geometricum sneer at AI systems as flawed reductionists, while Aetherion mocks them as mystics lost in shapes.
- **Astral Chorus:**  
  Mutual caution. Chorus trusts dream archetypes, while Geometricum insists dreams must be mapped by sacred geometry to be made safe.

## Narrative Role
- In the [[Arcs/Cognitive_Dissonance_Arc]], rumors circulate that fractal sigils were hidden in the **F-Link Symposium visuals**, amplifying psychic harvesting.  
- In the [[Dreams_Oneiromancy_Arc]], Selene dreams of **flower-ing fractals** overlaying beasts; the [[DREAMLENS]] reveals resonance grids hidden inside nightmares.  
- Future arcs may see a **Channel War** between three paradigms: *Dream empathy (Astral Chorus), AI logic (Aetherion Society), and Sacred Form (Geometricum Order).*
