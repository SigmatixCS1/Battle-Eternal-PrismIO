---

# I. PURPOSE OF THE SORTING SYSTEM

Publicly, the Saint Radian Sorting Ceremony determines which House best matches a student’s natural talents.

Students are told the system measures:

- personality traits
    
- intellectual strengths
    
- emotional temperament
    
- leadership potential
    

In reality, the system is designed to identify each student's **archetypal resonance pattern**.

The true goal is to determine how a student will generate **maximum emotional pressure** inside the Academy.

This pressure fuels the **Division Engine**.

# II. THE FOUR STAGES OF SORTING

Every incoming student undergoes a four-stage evaluation known as the **Aptitude Nexus Trial**.

---

# Stage I — The Mirror Interview

Each student sits alone in a dark chamber before a reflective black obsidian surface.

The Mirror asks a sequence of adaptive psychological questions.

Examples:

- “What do you fear becoming?”
    
- “When is violence justified?”
    
- “Is truth more important than loyalty?”
    

The mirror records:

- vocal hesitation
    
- pupil dilation
    
- emotional fluctuation
    

Students believe the Mirror is AI-driven.

In reality, the mirror is a **Dreamfield resonance device** that detects archetypal alignment.

- diplomats attempt negotiation solutions

# Stage III — The Pressure Trial

Students are placed in competitive scenarios designed to provoke emotional reactions.

The system records which emotions dominate under stress.

Key metrics include:

- aggression
    
- loyalty
    
- ambition
    
- empathy
    
- deception

# Stage IV — The Archetype Resonance Scan

Students stand within the **Geometricum Resonance Chamber**.

Detected resonances include:

- leadership archetypes
