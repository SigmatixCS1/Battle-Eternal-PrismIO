# The Saint Radian Sorting System

### (The Axiom Trial)

Every new student undergoes a ritual called:

**The Axiom Trial**

Publicly it is framed as:

> “A ceremonial aptitude evaluation revealing the student's natural calling.”

Secretly it is:

> **A metaphysical diagnostic scanning the soul for archetypal resonance.**

The trial determines which **House or Order** a student belongs to among the Saint Radian factions.

---

# 1️⃣ How Students Get Sorted

The ritual occurs during **Opening Night Convocation**.

Location:

**The Hall of Refractions**  
A cathedral-like chamber beneath Saint Radian.

Inside the hall:

- A **circular floor of living sigils**
    
- Nine hovering crystalline pillars
    
- A central artifact called:
    

### The Nexus Engine

Students step into the circle one at a time.

The system reads:

- emotional reactions
    
- moral instincts
    
- subconscious fears
    
- archetypal resonance
    

What the audience sees:

Visions.

What the Order sees:

**Data.**

# 2️⃣ The Three Tests of the Axiom Trial

The trial contains **three psychological phases**.

Each phase reveals a different layer of the soul.

---

# Test 1 — The Mirror of Self

A crystal projection forces the student to confront:

- their deepest fear
    
- their hidden desire
    
- their unresolved wound
    

Example visions:

- abandonment
    
- failure
    
- betrayal
    
- power
    

What the Order measures:

- **emotional volatility**
    
- **ego stability**
    
- **susceptibility to manipulation**
    

Why it matters:

Students with strong emotional reactions produce **more harvestable energy**.

---

# Test 2 — The Trial of Choice

The student faces a simulated scenario.

Examples:

- Save a friend or save a city.
    
- Obey authority or break the rule.
    
- Seek truth or protect harmony.
    

The student must decide instantly.

The system measures:

- moral reasoning
    
- leadership instinct
    
- rebellion threshold
    
- loyalty vs autonomy
    

This determines **House alignment**.
