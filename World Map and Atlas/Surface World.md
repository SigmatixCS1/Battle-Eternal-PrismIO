### Battle Eternal World Atlas

The **Surface World** of Battle Eternal in the year **2038** appears familiar to modern civilization, but beneath the illusion of normality lies a planetary system shaped by technological integration, religious upheaval, and hidden mythological forces. The world is divided into several major geopolitical regions, while deeper layers of reality operate through metaphysical structures unseen by most of humanity.

This document defines the **canonical geography and global power structure** of the Battle Eternal universe.

---

# Global Map Overview

The world is divided into **five major civilization blocs** and several **restricted mythological territories**.

## Civilization Regions

1. Pacific Nexus
2. Western Hegemony
3. Eurasian Dominion
4. Southern Coalition
5. Sacred Belt

## Hidden Regions

1. Forbidden Expanse
2. Dreamfield Convergence Zones
3. Fury Gateways
4. Mythic Territories

---

# Pacific Nexus

## Primary City

**Neo-Osaka**

Neo-Osaka is the most technologically advanced city in the world and serves as the global center of neural integration technology.

Key elements:
- headquarters of **F-Link neural infrastructure**
- location of **Saint Radian Institute**
- experimental Dreamfield research
- high concentration of advanced AI systems

The city functions as a **global technological nexus**, making it the most important strategic location on the planet.

### Cultural Identity

Neo-Osaka blends ancient spiritual symbolism with ultra-modern cybernetic infrastructure. Shrines and temples coexist with towering data centers and corporate megastructures.

---

# Western Hegemony

The Western Hegemony represents the most economically powerful political alliance in the world.

Characteristics:
- multinational corporate dominance
- financial control of global markets
- strong media influence
- advanced research universities

Major megacities:
- New Avalon
- Neo-London
- Pacifica

Hidden influence from global elite councils shapes many of the Hegemony's decisions.

---

# Eurasian Dominion

The Eurasian Dominion is a centralized authoritarian superpower.

Characteristics:
- strict digital surveillance
- strong military control
- state-managed neural integration
- limited civil freedoms

The Dominion views F-Link primarily as a **tool of governance and societal control**.

---

# Southern Coalition

The Southern Coalition is a diverse alliance of rapidly developing nations.

Characteristics:
- emerging technological hubs
- major population centers
- strong cultural and religious diversity

The region becomes a central battleground for ideological conflict once global prophecy movements emerge.

---

# Sacred Belt

The Sacred Belt is the world's largest concentration of historic religious centers.

Cities include:
- Neo-Venice
- São Paulo
- New Constantinople
- Alexandria Prime

During the early stages of F-Link deployment, many religious institutions reinterpret neural integration as a **new form of divine communion**.

## Communion Ritual

Traditional Eucharist rituals evolve into **Neural Communion**. Instead of bread and wine, worshippers receive a **micro-neural wafer implant**.

The ritual phrase:

> "Unify with God."

This transformation ignites major theological and political conflicts worldwide.

---

# Forbidden Expanse

Large regions of the planet remain officially restricted.

Public explanations include:
- environmental collapse
- extreme radiation
- military quarantine zones

The true reason is far more complex. These territories contain **ancient mythological sites and Dreamfield anomalies**.

Possible features include:
- Fury Gateways
- lost civilizations
- Dreamfield rupture zones
- unknown archetypal entities

These regions are largely unexplored and represent major future story arcs.

---

# F-Link Global Network

F-Link is a neural integration system designed to connect human consciousness into a planetary communication network.

Key components:
- neural wafer implant
- nanite integration
- global data synchronization

Major nodes appear in megacities around the world. When fully operational, the network forms a **planet-wide neural lattice**.

# Hidden Power Structures

Beneath global politics operate several secret organizations. These groups manipulate both governments and religious institutions to shape the course of human civilization. Most citizens remain unaware of their existence.

One of the most influential hidden powers is the **Order of the Black Sun**, which seeks to control the metaphysical infrastructure underlying the world.

---

# The Planetary Transformation

By the beginning of the story, three forces are converging simultaneously:

1. Technological Integration
2. Religious Prophecy
3. Mythological Awakening

The interaction between these forces leads to the catastrophic event known as the **Quantum Fracture**. This event destabilizes the boundary between the Surface World and deeper layers of reality.

---

# Narrative Importance

The world map serves as the foundation for all global story arcs.

Season One focuses primarily on:
- Neo-Osaka
- Saint Radian Institute
- early F-Link deployment

Future seasons gradually expand the scope to include global political conflicts and mythological territories.

---

# Canonical Map Notes

The world of Battle Eternal is intentionally designed to appear familiar while hiding deeper layers of truth. Human civilization occupies only part of the planet's true structure.

Beyond the known regions lie territories where myth, consciousness, and reality intersect. These places will become increasingly important as the story unfolds.
