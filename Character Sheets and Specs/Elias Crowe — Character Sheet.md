- Reflections may lag behind his movement

### Core Concept

Elias reduces his own age while generating multiple versions of himself across temporal states.

---

- Time fractures around him

- Each clone represents:

    - Slight behavioral and reaction differences
        
    - Unique timing and decision-making patterns

- Multiple clones active simultaneously
    
- Highly unpredictable combat behavior
