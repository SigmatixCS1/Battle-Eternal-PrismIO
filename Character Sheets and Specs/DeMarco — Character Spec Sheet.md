### Hair & Facial Details

- Completely bald head — smooth, gleaming scalp with subtle warm reflection
- Short, tightly trimmed boxed beard — sharp edges, full but controlled
- Thick, expressive eyebrows (slightly arched in dominance)
- Strong jawline, prominent cheekbones, intense narrowed gaze
- Small gold hoop earring (left ear) + thin gold chain necklace with pendant
- Expression baseline: confident smirk or predatory calm — never wide smile unless mocking

### Body & Pose Language

- Broad-shouldered, thick-necked, powerful frame — fills doorways
- Relaxed but coiled posture — hands in pockets or resting on surface like he owns it
- Default: standing with slight forward lean (intimidation), or seated wide-legged (claiming space)

### Signature Power Element

- Glowing golden sigil disc / forge energy ring — intricate geometric patterns
- Emerald-green energy trails when active (eye-matched)
- Forged-metal aesthetic — brass/gold with faint heat distortion
