# Saint Radian Campus Hidden Map

### Dual-Layer Institutional Structure

Saint Radian is deliberately designed as a **two-layer system**.

|Layer|Description|
|---|---|
|Public Layer|Houses, clubs, classrooms, dormitories|
|Hidden Layer|Research labs, Dreamfield facilities, Order operations|

Every House, Order, and Society operates with this structure:

```text
Public Identity (Student Culture)
        ↓
Hidden Function (Order Operations)
```

Students believe they are joining **clubs and academic societies**.

In reality they are being sorted into **specialized research divisions**.

# The Campus Structural Layout

### Surface Campus

The visible Saint Radian includes:

- The Grand Atrium (sorting ceremonies)
    
- Dorm towers
    
- Research libraries
    
- Training arenas
    
- Botanical gardens
    
- Academic halls
    

Each major House or Society occupies its own building.

---

### Subterranean Layer

Beneath the campus lies a massive hidden complex containing:

- Dreamfield containment labs
    
- archetype research vaults
    
- magical artifact storage
    
- mythic creature dissection facilities
    
- Division Engine infrastructure
    
- Order command chambers
    

These areas are accessed through **hidden elevators, sealed staircases, and sigil locks**.

Students only discover them if they are **initiated or recruited**.

# House / Society Dual Structure

Below is the real function of each faction.

---

# Order of the Obsidian Glyphs

**Public Identity:**  
Ancient language, sigil writing, archeological inscriptions.

Motto: _“In Shadows, Truth is Carved.”_

### Hidden Facility — The Archive Vault

A massive underground chamber filled with:

- forbidden grimoires
    
- lost mythological languages
    
- archetype binding rituals
    

Purpose:

Studying how **symbols bind archetypal forces**.

Their research often clashes with the tech-oriented Aetherion Society.

---

# Aetherion Society

**Public Identity:**  
Advanced engineering and AI development club.

Motto: _“Forge the Future, Bind the Past.”_

### Hidden Facility — The Aetherforge

A highly secret research laboratory.

Purpose:

Digitizing magic.

Projects include:

- converting sigil magic into programmable code
    
- integrating archetypal energy into F-Link systems
    
- synthesizing magical effects through technology
    

Traditionalists view this work as **deeply sacrilegious**.

---

# Astral Chorus

**Public Identity:**  
Dream interpretation and psychology studies.

Motto: _“Listen to the Unspoken.”_

### Hidden Facility — The Dream Chamber

A circular hall filled with meditation pools and resonance mirrors.

Purpose:

Training dreamwalkers to enter the **Dreamfield consciously**.

Astral Chorus members act as **living sensors for Dreamfield activity**.

Selene belongs to this faction.

---

# Order of the Crimson Flame

**Public Identity:**  
Martial arts and ritual dueling.

Motto: _“Burn the Veil.”_

### Hidden Facility — The War Sanctum

An underground arena used to train students to fight:

- mythic creatures
    
- Dreamfield entities
    
- hostile operatives
    

Kaelen spends much of his time here.

This Order believes **strength and discipline maintain balance**.

---

# House of the Liminal Veil

**Public Identity:**  
Philosophy, diplomacy, and political theory.

Motto: _“We Walk Between.”_

### Hidden Facility — The Veil Network

A shadow intelligence hub.

Members specialize in:

- infiltration
    
- surveillance
    
- information warfare
    

They also serve as **agents of the Furies**.

This makes them extremely dangerous.

---

# House of Living Sigils

**Public Identity:**  
Botany and ecological research.

Motto: _“Growth in the Ink.”_

### Hidden Facility — The Verdant Laboratory

An underground greenhouse used to cultivate:

- magical plants
    
- bio-sigil organisms
    
- Dreamfield-infused ecosystems
    

They believe the Primal Wilds should be **respected rather than exploited**.

Elara’s empathy with the Centaur aligns with this philosophy.

---

# Order of the Eternal Sol

**Public Identity:**  
Leadership, politics, and debate.

Motto: _“In Light, We Bind.”_

### Hidden Facility — The Solar Chamber

A strategic command center used to groom **future Order leaders**.

Their purpose is narrative control:

- shaping public myths
    
- influencing global institutions
    
- maintaining the illusion of order
    

DeMarco is a rising star within this Order.

---

# The Dialectic Order

**Public Identity:**  
Debate club and logic competitions.

Motto: _“Truth Through Argument.”_

### Hidden Facility — The Cognitive Lab

Researchers study:

- psychological manipulation
    
- persuasion techniques
    
- narrative engineering
    

They design many of the **information systems used by the Janglers**.

# Reactions to the Centaur Incident

### Internal Faction Reports

After the battle, each faction interprets the event differently.

---

# Obsidian Glyphs Report

**Conclusion:** Archetypal rupture.

They believe the Centaur was a **symbolic manifestation tied to mythic instability**.

Concern:

The Dreamfield boundary may be weakening.

Recommendation:

Increase archetype research.

---

# Aetherion Society Report

**Conclusion:** Energy opportunity.

They are fascinated by the Centaur’s biology.

Goal:

Extract mythic energy signatures and convert them into digital systems.

Some members openly say:

> “Imagine weaponizing this.”

---

# Astral Chorus Report

**Conclusion:** Ethical catastrophe.

They believe the Centaur was **a conscious being displaced from its home realm**.

They argue the Order should focus on **returning creatures rather than killing them**.

Selene and Elara are deeply disturbed by the incident.

---

# Crimson Flame Report

**Conclusion:** Combat success.

They view the incident as a training scenario.

Kaelen’s intervention is praised.

However they criticize DeMarco for **reckless magical escalation**.

---

# Liminal Veil Report

**Conclusion:** Unknown manipulation.

They suspect the breach may have been **intentionally triggered**.

Their agents begin quietly investigating the Order itself.

---

# Living Sigils Report

**Conclusion:** Ecological violation.

They believe the Primal Wilds are **being destabilized by human interference**.

Some members openly accuse the Order of **poaching mythic creatures**.

---

# Eternal Sol Report

**Conclusion:** Narrative risk.

Their main concern is **public perception**.

They begin planning strategies to suppress rumors.

DeMarco is quietly warned about his aggressive response.

---

# Dialectic Order Report

**Conclusion:** Psychological data windfall.

They analyze how students reacted during the panic.

Their research focuses on:

- fear behavior
    
- rumor spread
    
- authority response
    

They consider the event **valuable experimental data**.

---

The Centaur incident becomes **the first fracture in Saint Radian’s unity**.

Every faction interprets the event through its own ideology.

Which quietly escalates the **institutional cold war beneath the academy**.
