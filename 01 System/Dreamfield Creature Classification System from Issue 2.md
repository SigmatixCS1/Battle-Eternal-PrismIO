# Dreamfield Creature Classification System

_(Saint Radian Research Taxonomy)_

This system is how **Saint Radian researchers and the Order of the Black Sun categorize Dreamfield entities** when they breach into the Surface World.

The creatures originate from the **Primal Wilds**, a mythic ecosystem connected to the Dreamfield and Mythic Strata where archetypal beings exist as living consciousness frameworks.

These beings are not merely animals.

They are **embodied archetypes**.

---

# The Three Classes of Dreamfield Entities

## Class I — Echo Beasts

_(Weak manifestations)_

These are the most common Dreamfield entities.

They form when:

- emotional spikes
    
- collective fear
    
- dream instability
    

create temporary manifestations.

Characteristics:

• unstable physical form  
• fade after short duration  
• limited intelligence  
• symbolic behavior

Examples:

- shadow wolves
    
- nightmare insects
    
- phantom birds
    

Narrative role:

These creatures are **environmental hazards**, not major threats.

---

## Class II — Primal Beasts

_(The Centaur category)_

These are true **Primal Wilds entities**.

They are not hallucinations.

They are **conscious beings pulled across the Dreamfield boundary**.

Examples from your lore include:

- Centaurs (instinct / dual nature)
    
- Ceryneian Hind (impossible pursuit)
    
- Kitsune (adaptive intelligence)
    
- Phoenix / Hō-ō (rebirth archetype)
    

Characteristics:

• extremely powerful  
• highly intelligent  
• symbolic consciousness  
• hostile when displaced

Most importantly:

They are **not evil**.

They are **out of place**.

Which is exactly the emotional reveal Elara experiences during the Centaur scene.

---

## Class III — Archetype Avatars

_(Rare entities)_

These are **fully conscious mythic intelligences**.

They are not just beasts.

They are **representatives of archetypal forces**.

Examples:

- Cerberus (guardian archetype)
    
- Hō-ō (phoenix archetype)
    
- Leviathan-class sea entities
    
- Titan consciousness fragments
    

These beings usually **do not enter the Surface World directly**.

They interact through dreams or chosen vessels.
